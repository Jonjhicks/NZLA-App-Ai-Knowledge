# How long does Fusarium treatment take to work?

Stops spreading within days. Visible recovery takes weeks.

What the fungicide actually does:

**Fungus Pro (contact protectant)** works on the fungal mycelium it touches and protects healthy tissue from new infection. Stops the disease spreading further within 24 to 48 hours of application.

**Azoxy (systemic)** moves into the leaf and disrupts the fungus already inside the plant. Stops active progression within 3 to 5 days of application.

What the lawn does in response:

Existing dead patches stay dead. The fungicide doesn't bring dead tissue back to life. Those patches stay brown until grass grows over them.

Active expansion of the disease stops once the fungicide has taken effect. Patches that were spreading stabilise.

New patches stop appearing (assuming the cultural conditions are also being addressed).

Visible recovery starts when conditions allow new growth to fill the dead patches. In warm growing conditions, that's 4 to 6 weeks. In winter cool conditions, recovery waits for spring temperatures.

Timeline expectations:

Day 1-3 after first fungicide application: disease stabilises but visible damage unchanged.

Week 1-2: no new patches forming, existing patches not enlarging.

Week 3-4: depending on conditions, grass starts filling in around the edges of dead patches. In winter, no visible change yet.

Week 6-12: most patches filled in (warm season recovery). Winter recovery starts when spring returns.

If active disease continues after fungicide application:

Cultural causes not addressed (continuing evening watering, no dew removal, soft N growth).

Resistance to the fungicide (if same chemistry has been used repeatedly without rotation).

Wrong identification (the symptoms might be a different disease).

Insufficient application (poor coverage, low water volume, washed off by rain too soon).

The rotation pattern:

Fungus Pro every 4 weeks (every 2 weeks in high humidity) as protectant.

Azoxy at first sign of active disease for systemic knockdown. Maximum 2 consecutive applications before rotating to Fungus Pro.

The two chemistries together cover prevention and cure across a winter season. Single fungicide use without rotation accelerates resistance and reduces effectiveness over time.

# How do I prevent dollar spot?

Dollar spot is a low-fertility disease. The single most useful prevention is keeping the lawn properly fed.

The reason. Dollar spot thrives on lawns where the grass is growing slowly because it's underfed. Slow-growing grass can't outgrow the disease — the small dollar-sized patches build up faster than the lawn fills back over them. A well-fed lawn grows steadily, the disease can't keep up, and even where it appears the lawn outgrows it within days.

The standard prevention programme:

**Steady fertility.** NZLA All Seasons every 6 to 8 weeks through the growing season provides balanced nutrition with moderate nitrogen. Avoids both the underfed state that invites dollar spot and the overfed state that invites Fusarium. All Seasons is the value-priced alternative.

**Iron and potassium for hardiness.** The 5% iron and 16% potassium in All Seasons (or 6% iron in All Seasons) harden the plant against multiple disease pressures. Iron+ foliar through cooler months supports this.

**Reduce leaf wetness.** Dew removal in the morning. Water in the morning rather than evening. Improve airflow where possible. Same cultural moves as for other foliar diseases.

**Mow regularly with sharp blades.** Removes potentially infected leaf tips, reduces tearing damage that creates infection entry points.

What NOT to do:

Don't underfeed. Holding back fertiliser in fear of disease misses the point — dollar spot is a low-fertility disease. Underfeeding makes it worse, not better.

Don't push high N late in the season. The disease pressure spike from soft growth into cool damp conditions invites Fusarium (a different disease but worse outcome). Balanced fertility through the growing season is the answer.

Don't water in the evening. Standard rule but specifically relevant for dollar spot's preference for high humidity nights.

If dollar spot appears despite good prevention:

Cultural intervention first — increase fertility (Charger foliar between granular applications), reduce leaf wetness more aggressively. Dollar spot often clears within 2 to 3 weeks with cultural fix alone.

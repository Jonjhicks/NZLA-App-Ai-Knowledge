# How do I stop possums damaging my lawn?

Two angles: remove the food reward and discourage the possums directly.

Food reward (the grubs):
- NZLA Grub+ at the right time controls grass grub larvae before they become a food source.
- Standard timing is late summer to autumn (February to April in most regions) targeting small larvae.
- Once grub populations drop, possums find easier feeding elsewhere.

Direct deterrents:
- Motion-activated sprinklers (commercial garden products) startle possums and discourage repeat visits.
- Outdoor lighting on a timer or motion sensor reduces nocturnal activity in the affected area.
- Removing access points (overhanging tree branches, fences they walk along) limits possum entry to the property.

Trapping:
- Cage traps baited with apple or peanut butter are effective and humane.
- Trapped possums must be dispatched (it's illegal to relocate them in NZ - they're a declared pest).
- Hardware stores sell traps; councils sometimes loan them.
- DOC has guidance on humane dispatch methods.

Bait stations:
- Cyanide-based baits (such as Feratox) are highly effective on possums.
- Anticoagulant baits (such as brodifacoum) also work but have non-target risks.
- Both require proper handling, secure bait stations away from pets and kids, and following label requirements precisely.
- Some councils run possum control programmes - check what's available in your area.

Long-term:
- Possum populations in NZ suburban areas are well established. Single-property control reduces pressure but doesn't eliminate the source.
- Where neighbours are also dealing with possum issues, coordinated trapping or baiting is more effective than individual efforts.

Honest framing:
- The lawn damage is usually about the grubs, not the possums per se.
- Address the grub issue and the possum visits drop.
- Direct possum control on top of grub management gives the cleanest result.
- Repairing the lawn before the underlying pest issue is sorted means doing the same work again next autumn.

For typical NZ home lawn possum damage, a Grub+ application timed correctly plus repair of the torn-up areas usually resolves the visible problem. Direct possum control is a separate decision based on broader pest pressure on the property.

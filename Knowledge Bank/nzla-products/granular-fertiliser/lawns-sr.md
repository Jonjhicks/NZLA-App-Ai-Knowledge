# NZLA All Seasons (Lawns SR)

Slow-release granular base fertiliser. NPK 20-0-16 with 6% iron, polymer-coated urea technology with an 8 to 10 week release duration. The value-priced companion to NZLA All Seasons in the granular range.

## Programme status — important

This product is no longer part of the standard NZLA recommendation programme. Per Jon's direction and the NZLA Application Guide:

- **Spring push applications (September, October, November):** All Seasons N+ is now the standard recommendation.
- **Autumn and general maintenance (March, May, other applications):** All Seasons is now the standard recommendation.

For existing customers using Lawns SR, the product information below remains accurate. For new customers or anyone building a programme from scratch, direct them to All Seasons and All Seasons N+ instead.

## What the product is

Polymer-coated urea delivering 20% nitrogen, 0% phosphorus, and 16% potassium steadily over 8 to 10 weeks. The 6% iron content is slightly higher than All Seasons (5%), giving a marginally stronger colour response. No phosphorus in the formulation — not usually relevant on established NZ lawns where P is rarely deficient.

Rate is 2.5 kg per 100m². A 10 kg bag covers 500m².

## How it compares to All Seasons

Very similar in profile and technology. All Seasons (21-1-16, 5% iron, 6 to 8 week release) and this product (20-0-16, 6% iron, 8 to 10 week release) are close enough that the practical difference on most lawns is minimal. The main distinction is price point and pack size — Lawns SR was the value-priced 10 kg bag option; All Seasons is available in 4 kg, 10 kg, and 20 kg.

The longer release window (8 to 10 vs 6 to 8 weeks) means slightly slower initial colour response but a longer sustained feed between applications.

## For current Lawns SR users

Same application windows as All Seasons — early spring, late spring, autumn, optional late autumn at reduced rate. Apply to moist soil and water in with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation-general within 24 hours.

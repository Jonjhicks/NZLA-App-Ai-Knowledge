# What's the application rate for NZLA Starter?

1.5 kg per 100 m² (15 g per square metre). Pack sizes are 4 kg (covers around 270 m²) and 7.5 kg (covers around 500 m²), suited to typical home lawn establishment projects.

Practical application:
- Spread evenly across prepared soil with a spreader or by hand on small areas.
- Rake or roll into the top 25 to 50 mm of soil so the phosphorus is in the seedling root zone.
- Sow seed or lay turf on top.
- Water in with light irrigation.

For overseeding or patch repair where you can't incorporate into the soil, spread at the same rate on the surface and rake in lightly before sowing seed. Phosphorus on the surface is less effective than incorporated phosphorus, but it's better than nothing on a thin or worn lawn that needs root push.

Don't double the rate hoping for faster establishment. Excess phosphorus and nitrogen can damage germinating seedlings and encourage weed pressure. The label rate balances root development with seedling safety.

One application at establishment, then switch to standard maintenance feeds (All Seasons or All Seasons N+) once the lawn is 6 to 8 weeks old and being mown regularly.

# What's the rate for All Seasons N+?

Check the current N+ label for rate. Working figure is around 2 kg per 100 m², similar to All Seasons, with adjustments for the specific use case.

For full push (spring thickening, post-renovation recovery): 2 kg per 100 m². One bag covers 1000 m².

For lighter recovery feeds (post-disease, modest growth push): 1 to 1.5 kg per 100 m². Less push, less burn risk on stressed turf.

Water in straight after application, 5 to 10 mm of irrigation or rainfall within 24 hours. Higher nitrogen rates without watering in are a recipe for surface burn.

Use a spreader and split the rate (half north-south, half east-west) for even coverage. Striped lawns from uneven granular application are particularly visible with high-N products because the response gradient is sharper.

Don't double the rate to get a faster response. The release profile is engineered for the rate it's designed at, doubling doesn't double the result, it increases the burn risk and waste.

Once the lawn has responded (typically 2 to 4 weeks), assess whether another N+ application is needed or whether the lawn is back to baseline and All Seasons can take over for the maintenance cycle.

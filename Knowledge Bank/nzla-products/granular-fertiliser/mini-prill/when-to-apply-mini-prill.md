# When should I apply Mini Prill?

When you need a fast nitrogen push and the canopy is dense or the cutting height is low. Standard timings:

- Spring (October to November) push.
- Late summer (February) push.
- Post-renovation feed (once seed is established 4 to 6 weeks).
- Recovery from disease, drought, wear.

Mini Prill is 70% immediately available with a 30% controlled-release tail. That gives fast initial response (within a week of watering in) and a sustained push for another 4 to 6 weeks.

Apply every 10 to 12 weeks during the growing season. Don't stack applications closer than that, the high nitrogen content can burn if it builds up.

Apply to well-watered turf, water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation or rainfall.

Avoid:
- Drought-stressed lawns.
- Going into winter (soft growth in cold conditions invites disease).
- Lawns under heavy disease pressure already.

For a typical home lawn at 35 to 50 mm cutting height, this product is overkill, All Seasons N+ at standard prill size will do the same job. Reserve Mini Prill for the specific situations where prill size genuinely matters.

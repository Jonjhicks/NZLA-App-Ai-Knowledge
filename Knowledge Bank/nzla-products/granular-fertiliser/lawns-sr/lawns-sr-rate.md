# What's the application rate for All Seasons?

2.5 kg per 100 m² (25 g per square metre). One 10 kg bag covers 500 m².

Use a spreader and split the rate (half north-south, half east-west) for even coverage. Calibrate the spreader before the first run, the setting markings on the spreader are a starting point not a guarantee of accuracy.

Water in straight after application, 5 to 10 mm of irrigation or rainfall within 24 hours starts the release working.

For lighter feeds (recovery applications, late autumn maintenance), drop to 1.5 kg per 100 m². Still gives reasonable response without overdoing it.

Don't exceed label rate. Slow-release products release at the rate they're designed for, doubling the application doesn't double the response, it just wastes product and increases burn risk if the coating fails on any granules.

For a standard home lawn programme, 3 to 4 applications of All Seasons per year at 2.5 kg per 100 m² provides decent year-round nitrogen and potassium with consistent colour. Combine with foliar applications of Iron+, Liquid N or Liquid Boost between granular applications for finer control of colour and growth response.

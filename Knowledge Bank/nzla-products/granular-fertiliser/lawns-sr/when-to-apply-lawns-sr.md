# When should I apply All Seasons?

Same windows as All Seasons. Three to four applications a year:

- Early spring (September)
- Late spring to early summer (November)
- Autumn (March to April)
- Late autumn (May, optional, light rate)

All Seasons has a slightly longer release period than All Seasons (8 to 10 weeks vs 6 to 8). That gives a bit more room between applications and a smoother feeding curve through the season.

Apply to well-watered turf, water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation or rainfall.

Avoid the height of summer in heat-stressed regions. Wait until the lawn is actively growing again before applying.

Same approach as All Seasons in terms of timing logic, just with a slightly longer release window. The choice between the two comes down to nitrogen ratio (All Seasons is 20-0-16 vs All Seasons 21-1-16), iron content (6% vs 5%), and price point rather than timing.

# What's the application rate for Grub+?

Check the Grub+ label for the exact current rate. Typical liquid insecticide rates for turf grub control are in the range of 20 to 40 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water, depending on the formulation.

Practical points:
- Water the lawn deeply the day before application. Hydrated soil is essential for the active to move down to where soil-dwelling pests are feeding.
- Apply with even coverage. Carrier water 4 to 10 L per 100 m².
- For soil-feeding pests (grass grub, black beetle), water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation or rainfall. The active needs to reach the soil to contact the larvae.
- For surface-feeding pests (porina, crickets, sod webworms), light watering only after application, or apply in the evening with no irrigation. The active needs to stay where the pest is feeding.

One application per pest cycle is usually enough. Repeat applications aren't recommended within the same season for the same pest, label rates are set for a reason.

If you've got multiple pest problems in the same lawn at the same time, one Grub+ application timed for the most pressing pest usually catches the others.

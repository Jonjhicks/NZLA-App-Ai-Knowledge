# When should I apply Liquid Starter?

After germination, at 7 to 10 day intervals through the establishment phase. Standard programme:

- Day 7 to 10 after germination, when seedlings show first true leaves.
- Day 17 to 20, second application.
- Day 27 to 30, third application.
- Week 5 to 6, fourth application (optional, transition to Liquid Boost if continuing foliar push).

Apply with a gentle spray (fine rose watering can on small areas, low-pressure sprayer on larger areas). Don't blast seedlings with a high-pressure spray, you'll knock them around.

Conditions:
- Soil and air temps 14 °C or warmer for active uptake.
- Calm conditions, no wind.
- Apply early morning or late afternoon (avoid hot midday).
- Light irrigation 2 to 4 hours after application is fine, washes any product on the soil down to the root zone.

Don't apply:
- Immediately after sowing (before germination, granular Starter is doing the work).
- To stressed or wilted seedlings (water first).
- During hot dry windy conditions.

For overseeding and patch repair, same approach. Apply Liquid Starter 7 to 10 days after new seedlings emerge, repeat at 10 day intervals until seedlings are well established.

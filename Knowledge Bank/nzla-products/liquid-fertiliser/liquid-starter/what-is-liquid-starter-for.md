# What is Liquid Starter used for?

Foliar feed for new lawn establishment. Designed to push seedling growth on top of an NZLA Starter granular base.

Where it fits:
- Days 7 to 10 after seed germination, once the first true leaves are showing.
- Repeat applications at 7 to 10 day intervals through the establishment phase (first 4 to 6 weeks).
- Tank-mix component for combined applications with Amino+ or Humic+ for seedling biology support.
- Patch repair situations to accelerate fill-in.
- Overseeding existing lawn to accelerate establishment of the new seedlings.

The product is formulated for the seedling stage, with nutrient ratios suited to early plant development. Lower burn risk than mature lawn fertilisers, suited to small immature root systems and tender leaves.

Compared to NZLA Starter (granular):
- Granular goes into the soil before sowing, supplies phosphorus to the root zone for 12 weeks.
- Liquid goes on the seedlings after germination, supplies nutrition through the leaf for rapid above-ground push.
- The two work together rather than substituting for each other.

For a strong new lawn programme, use both. NZLA Starter incorporated at soil prep, then Liquid Starter applied at 7 to 10 day intervals through the first 4 to 6 weeks after germination.

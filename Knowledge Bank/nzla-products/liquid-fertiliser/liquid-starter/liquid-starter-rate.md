# What's the application rate for Liquid Starter?

Check the current Liquid Starter label for the exact rate. Working figure for seedling applications is around 30 to 50 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

Lower rates for the first application (small seedlings, lower nutrient demand), higher rates as seedlings mature through the establishment phase.

Practical points:
- Use a fine-spray applicator. Fine rose watering can on tiny areas, gentle sprayer on larger areas.
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves where possible (early morning after dew has dried).
- Light watering 2 to 4 hours after application is acceptable (different to mature lawn foliar applications where you don't water in for 24 hours).
- Don't apply at higher than label rate, young seedlings are more vulnerable to burn than mature turf.

Tank-mix combinations for new lawn use:
- Liquid Starter + Amino+ = nutrition plus stress tolerance
- Liquid Starter + Humic+ = nutrition plus soil biology development
- Don't tank-mix with herbicide or fungicide unless specifically called for (seedlings are more vulnerable to chemical stress than mature turf).

For a 250 m² new lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 100 to 120 mL per application. Across the four applications in the establishment phase, that's about 500 mL total per 250 m² of new lawn.

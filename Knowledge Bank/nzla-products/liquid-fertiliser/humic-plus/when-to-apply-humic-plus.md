# When should I apply Humic+?

Through the growing season at monthly intervals if running as a programme. Specific use during establishment and after heavy chemical inputs.

Programme approach: monthly through spring, summer and autumn. Tank-mixed with nitrogen feeds or applied as a standalone soil drench.

Specific situations:
- At lawn establishment, after sowing or laying turf (helps young soil biology develop).
- Post-renovation (after scarifying, dethatching, core aeration).
- After fungicide or insecticide applications (helps soil biology recover).
- On sandy or low-organic-matter soils where the lawn is struggling.
- Pre-summer to help the lawn handle heat and drought stress.

Soil temps 10 °C or warmer for biology to respond. Cooler conditions still allow chemical benefits (pH buffering, nutrient chelation) but biological response is slower.

Unlike most foliar products, Humic+ can be watered in. The benefit is in the soil, so movement into the soil is helpful. Apply, then irrigate within 24 hours with 5 to 10 mm. Tank-mix with NZLA Aerate or Wetter for combined soil treatment.

For a typical home lawn, monthly Humic+ applications through the growing season noticeably improve soil structure and biology over a season or two. Not a quick-result product, more of a long-term lawn health investment.

# What's the application rate for Humic+?

Check the current Humic+ label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water.

Practical points:
- Higher carrier water volume than typical foliar applications (this is soil-targeted).
- Can be watered in after application (unlike foliar feeds).
- Tank-mix friendly with most NZLA liquid products.
- Routine monthly use at the lower rate, programme establishment or recovery at upper rate.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Humic+ + Amino+ = full biostimulant package
- Humic+ + Iron+ = soil chemistry support plus colour (the humic acid helps keep iron plant-available)
- Humic+ + Wetter or Aerate = soil treatment combined application
- Humic+ + Liquid N or Charger = nitrogen plus soil conditioning

Don't tank-mix with strongly alkaline products. Humic acid is mildly acidic and can react with high-pH formulations.

Watering in after application moves the humic acid into the soil where it's working. 5 to 10 mm of irrigation is typical. Heavy irrigation (over 20 mm) can dilute the chemistry through the profile too quickly.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 150 to 200 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers about 25 applications, roughly two seasons of monthly use.

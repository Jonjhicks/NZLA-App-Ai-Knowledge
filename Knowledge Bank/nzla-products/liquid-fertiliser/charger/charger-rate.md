# What's the application rate for Charger?

Check the current Charger label for the exact rate. Working figure for foliar applications is around 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water, depending on the desired response intensity.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even leaf coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves with a calibrated sprayer.
- Don't water in for 24 hours, this is foliar uptake.
- No mowing for 24 hours either side.
- Tank-mix friendly with Iron+, Humic+, Amino+, Wetter.

For a strong monthly push: upper end of the rate range.
For a maintenance feed: lower end of the rate range.
For combined applications with Iron+ (colour at the same time): mid-range rate of each.

Don't double the rate to extend the interval. Foliar uptake is rate-limited, the excess runs off or sits on the leaf without being absorbed. Stick to label rates at monthly intervals rather than double-rate at six-week intervals.

For a typical 250 m² home lawn at the mid-rate, you're using about 150 to 200 mL per application, so a 5 L bottle of Charger covers about 25 to 30 applications, roughly two full seasons at monthly intervals.

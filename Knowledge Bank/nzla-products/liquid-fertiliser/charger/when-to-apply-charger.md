# When should I apply Charger?

Through the main growing season, monthly intervals. Spring through autumn (October to April for cool-season turf).

Sample programme:
- October: first Charger application as the lawn comes out of winter
- November: second application
- December: third application
- January: hold or apply at half rate depending on heat stress
- February: resume monthly applications
- March: continue
- April: last application of the season before winter slowdown

Apply to a dry lawn with a calibrated sprayer at label rate in 4 to 8 L water per 100 m². Don't water in, foliar uptake needs the product on the leaf for several hours.

No mowing for 24 hours either side of application.

Don't apply to:
- Drought-stressed turf
- Heat-stressed turf (above 28 °C with no irrigation)
- Diseased lawn under active infection (push the recovery later)
- Very wet lawn (rain forecast within 6 hours)

Tank-mix friendly. Combine with Iron+ for colour, Humic+ or Amino+ for soil biology, NZLA PGR if running growth regulation.

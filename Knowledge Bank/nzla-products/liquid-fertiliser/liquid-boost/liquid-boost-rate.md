# What's the application rate for Liquid Boost?

Check the current Liquid Boost label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 30 to 50 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water, depending on the desired push intensity.

For a strong push (recovery from significant damage, pre-event push): upper end of the rate range.

For a maintenance top-up or supplement: lower end of the rate range.

Apply to dry leaves with a calibrated sprayer. Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m².

Don't water in for 24 hours, foliar uptake needs the product on the leaf.

No mowing for 24 hours either side of application.

Tank-mix friendly. Useful combinations:
- Liquid Boost + Iron+ = nitrogen push plus colour boost
- Liquid Boost + Amino+ = nitrogen plus biology support
- Liquid Boost + Wetter = push application with improved coverage in dry conditions

Don't tank-mix with PGR. The whole point of Liquid Boost is push response, the PGR works against that.

Two applications 7 to 10 days apart can be used for stronger recovery situations. More frequent than that and you're double-feeding without benefit.

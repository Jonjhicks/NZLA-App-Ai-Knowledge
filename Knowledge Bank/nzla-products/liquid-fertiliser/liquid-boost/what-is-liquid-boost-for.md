# What is Liquid Boost used for?

Quick response foliar nitrogen for push feeds and recovery. The product gives a fast visible result, you'll see colour and growth response within a few days.

Where it fits:
- Recovery from disease damage (after the disease is controlled).
- Recovery from drought stress (water first, then push).
- Post-renovation push once seedlings are 4 to 6 weeks old.
- Pre-event sharpening (wedding, garden party, putting the lawn at its best for a particular date).
- Late summer rust knock-back combined with Fungus Pro.
- Filling in patches after pest damage.

Compared to granular feeds: faster response, smaller dose per application, used episodically rather than monthly.

Compared to Charger: faster, shorter residual (2 to 3 weeks vs 3 to 4 weeks), better for episodic use rather than as a programme base.

One Liquid Boost application doesn't replace the annual granular programme, it sits alongside it. Use it when the lawn needs a push beyond what the granular base is doing, not as a substitute for regular maintenance feeds.

Tank-mix friendly with most foliar products. Combine with Iron+ for colour and Amino+ or Humic+ for soil and biology support.

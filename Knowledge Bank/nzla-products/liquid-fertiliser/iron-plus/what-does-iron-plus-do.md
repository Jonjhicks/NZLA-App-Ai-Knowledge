# What does NZLA Iron+ do?

Iron+ is a chelated iron foliar feed for deep green colour. Iron is the nutrient that drives the dark green tone in lawn turf. Without enough plant-available iron, lawns look pale or yellowish even with adequate nitrogen.

The chelated form is what makes Iron+ work well. Free iron in soil and water rapidly oxidises and becomes unavailable to plants. Chelating the iron (binding it to an organic molecule like EDTA) keeps it in a plant-available form long enough to be absorbed through the leaf or root.

What you'll see:
- Dark green colour response within 1 to 3 days of application.
- No growth push (Iron+ doesn't have meaningful nitrogen).
- Response lasts 2 to 4 weeks depending on cutting frequency.
- Iron is mowed off with the leaf, so frequent mowing shortens the response.

What it doesn't do:
- Make the lawn thicker (that's nitrogen).
- Make the lawn healthier (iron addresses one specific nutrient).
- Fix an underlying nutrient deficiency long-term (foliar iron is a top-up, not a soil correction).

For pre-event sharpening (wedding, garden party, photo shoot), Iron+ is the single most useful product in the range. Applied a week before the event, the lawn looks at its best on the day.

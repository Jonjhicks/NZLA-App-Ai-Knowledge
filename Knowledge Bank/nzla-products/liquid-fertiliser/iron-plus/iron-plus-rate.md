# What's the application rate for Iron+?

Check the current Iron+ label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 30 to 60 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

For a strong colour push (pre-event, deficiency correction): upper end of the rate range.
For routine programme use: lower end.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even leaf coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves.
- Don't water in for 24 hours.
- No mowing for 24 hours either side (the iron is on the leaf, mowing removes it).
- Watch for splash onto paths, paving, concrete - iron stains hard surfaces.

Tank-mix friendly:
- Iron+ + Liquid N or Liquid Boost = colour plus growth response in one application
- Iron+ + Charger = colour as part of a monthly foliar programme
- Iron+ + Fungus Pro + Liquid N = standard rust treatment combo

If you've stained concrete or paving, rinse with water as soon as possible. Set-in iron stains are hard to remove. Citric acid or proprietary rust removers can shift older stains.

For a typical 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using about 100 to 120 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 40 applications, which is roughly two seasons of monthly use.

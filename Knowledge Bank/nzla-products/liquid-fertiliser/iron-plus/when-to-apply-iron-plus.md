# When should I apply Iron+?

Whenever the lawn needs colour. Through the growing season (October to April for cool-season turf), at intervals of 3 to 4 weeks if you want consistent strong colour, or as one-off applications before events.

Soil and air temps 14 °C or warmer for active uptake. Iron+ works in cooler conditions too but the response is slower.

Specific use:
- Routine 3 to 4 week applications for a consistently dark green lawn.
- Pre-event single application (1 week before, you'll have peak colour on the day).
- Tank-mix component with nitrogen feeds for combined growth and colour push.
- Rust treatment partner with Fungus Pro and Liquid N.
- Recovery from yellowing caused by iron deficiency on alkaline or high-pH soils.

Avoid:
- Concrete or paved surfaces. Iron+ stains. Spray when winds are calm, don't drip onto paths or hard surfaces.
- Drought-stressed lawn (water first).
- Heat-stressed lawn above 28 °C with no irrigation.
- Light-coloured clothing while spraying (iron stains fabric).

Apply with a calibrated sprayer to dry leaves. No watering in for 24 hours. No mowing for 24 hours either side.

For a typical home lawn, monthly Iron+ applications through the growing season keep the lawn at peak colour. For event-driven use, a single application a week ahead does the job.

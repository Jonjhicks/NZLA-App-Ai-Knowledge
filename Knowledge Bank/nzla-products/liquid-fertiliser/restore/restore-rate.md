# What's the application rate for Restore?

Check the current Restore label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 30 to 50 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

For strong recovery push (significant damage, post-disease, post-renovation): upper end of the rate range.
For maintenance recovery (mild stress, ongoing programme support): lower end.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even leaf coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves.
- Don't water in for 24 hours.
- No mowing for 24 hours either side.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Restore + Liquid Boost or Liquid N = recovery push plus nitrogen
- Restore + Iron+ = recovery plus colour
- Restore + Amino+ = combined biostimulation and recovery (overlap, both products work toward similar outcome)

Don't double the rate to extend the interval. Foliar uptake is rate-limited at the leaf surface. Better to stick to label rates at 7 to 10 day intervals than double-rate at fortnightly intervals.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 80 to 100 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 50 applications, more than enough for the occasional recovery situation on a home lawn.

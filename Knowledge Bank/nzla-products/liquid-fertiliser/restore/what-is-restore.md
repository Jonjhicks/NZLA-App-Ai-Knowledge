# What is NZLA Restore?

Restore is a recovery-focused foliar product. Designed for use after stress events, the formulation supports the lawn's ability to bounce back from damage faster than it would unaided.

Where it fits:
- Post-disease recovery (after fungicide has stopped the infection).
- Post-renovation push (after scarifying, dethatching, core aeration).
- Recovery from drought, heat or wear stress.
- After herbicide application that's stressed the turf.
- Patch repair situations where you're trying to fill in damaged areas faster.

The product overlaps with Amino+ in some uses but Restore is positioned more directly as a recovery tool. The chemistry supports cell repair, root regeneration, and rapid tissue regrowth, where Amino+ is broader biostimulation that includes prevention as well as recovery.

Programme positioning:
- Single application at the start of recovery (1 to 7 days after the stress event has resolved).
- Repeat at 7 to 10 day intervals through the recovery phase (typically 2 to 4 applications).
- Tank-mix with Liquid Boost or Liquid N for combined nutrition and recovery support.

Not a substitute for addressing the underlying problem. If the lawn is recovering from disease, the fungicide is what stopped the disease; Restore helps the lawn grow back. If recovering from drought, water is what restored hydration; Restore helps the lawn rebuild from there.

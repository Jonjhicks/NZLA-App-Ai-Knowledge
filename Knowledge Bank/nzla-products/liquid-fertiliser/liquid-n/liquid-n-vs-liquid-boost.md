# Should I use Liquid N or Liquid Boost?

Two foliar nitrogen options with overlapping use.

Liquid N: pure urea-based foliar feed. Cheapest option per litre. Fastest response (1 to 2 weeks). Shortest residual.

Liquid Boost: nitrogen-based formulation with additional ingredients for stronger and longer response. Mid-range residual (2 to 3 weeks). Better for combined push scenarios.

Decision tree:
- Quick simple nitrogen hit, lowest cost: Liquid N.
- Stronger and longer push, better integration with other foliar products: Liquid Boost.
- Tank-mixing with Iron+ for rust treatment: either works, Liquid N is the cheaper option.
- Recovery from significant damage or stress: Liquid Boost.
- Programme use through the season: Charger (proper programme base) rather than either of these.

For home lawn occasional use, Liquid N covers most needs at the lowest cost. Liquid Boost makes sense when you want stronger or longer response than straight urea delivers.

For high-input lawns running foliar-led programmes, the typical structure is Charger as the monthly base, Liquid Boost for episodic pushes when needed, Liquid N as a cheap top-up when neither of the others is the right fit.

# What is Liquid N used for?

Fast-acting foliar nitrogen for response. Pure urea-based foliar feed, taken up through the leaf within hours, growth and colour response within days.

Where it fits:
- Spot fixes for nitrogen-poor areas.
- Quick colour push when the lawn is looking pale.
- Rust treatment alongside Fungus Pro (the nitrogen push helps the lawn grow past the damage).
- Tank-mix component for compound applications where you want extra nitrogen.
- Between granular feeds when you want a foliar top-up.

The faster response comes with shorter duration. Liquid N effect typically lasts 1 to 2 weeks. Compared to Liquid Boost (2 to 3 weeks) or Charger (3 to 4 weeks), it's the shortest residual of the foliar nitrogen options.

For most home lawn use, Liquid Boost handles the same situations with longer residual and better integration with other foliar products. Liquid N is the simpler, cheaper, fastest option when you want a quick nitrogen hit without the additional ingredients of Boost or the programme orientation of Charger.

Tank-mix friendly with Iron+, Amino+, Humic+, Wetter. Standard partner for Iron+ when you want both colour and growth response in one application.

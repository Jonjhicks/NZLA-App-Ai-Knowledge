# What's the application rate for Liquid N?

Check the Liquid N label for the current rate. Working figure for foliar urea is around 30 to 60 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

For a mild push (subtle colour, light nitrogen): lower end of the rate range.
For a strong response (rust treatment, deficiency correction): upper end.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even leaf coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves.
- Don't water in for 24 hours.
- No mowing for 24 hours either side.
- Burn risk goes up with hot weather and concentrated rates. Drop to lower end on a hot day.

Tank-mix friendly. Standard combinations:
- Liquid N + Iron+ = nitrogen plus colour
- Liquid N + Fungus Pro = rust treatment plus nitrogen push
- Liquid N + Amino+ = nitrogen plus biology support

Don't tank-mix with PGR. The PGR is holding growth back, the Liquid N is trying to push growth, they work against each other.

For a small lawn or spot use, hand-mixing a watering-can dose works fine. Calculate the rate based on the area you're actually treating, not the bottle dilution.

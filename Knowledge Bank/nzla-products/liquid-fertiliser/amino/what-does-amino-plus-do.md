# What does NZLA Amino+ do?

Amino+ is a biostimulant containing plant-available amino acids. Amino acids are the building blocks plants use to make proteins, and providing them in foliar form gives the plant a head-start, the lawn doesn't have to synthesise them from scratch.

What you'll see:
- Improved stress recovery (heat, drought, disease, mechanical damage)
- Stronger root system over time
- Better tolerance to chemical applications (fungicide, herbicide stress)
- Modest colour and density improvement
- Better soil biology over a programme of applications

It's not a direct fertiliser, the nitrogen content is low. The benefit is biostimulation, not nutrient delivery. Use Amino+ alongside nitrogen feeds (Charger, Liquid N, Liquid Boost) rather than instead of them.

Where Amino+ fits best:
- Pre-stress hardening (before summer heat, before a renovation, before disease season).
- Post-stress recovery (after disease, drought, renovation, herbicide application).
- High-input lawn programmes where you're pushing the lawn hard and need biology and stress tolerance to keep up.
- Soils with low organic matter and weak biology, where Humic+ partners well with Amino+.

For a typical home lawn, monthly Amino+ applications through the growing season add resilience that pays off in stress conditions. Not essential for a basic lawn programme but a strong addition for higher-end care.

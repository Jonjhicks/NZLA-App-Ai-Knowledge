# What's the application rate for Amino+?

Check the current Amino+ label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 30 to 60 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m² for even leaf coverage.
- Apply to dry leaves.
- Don't water in for 24 hours, this is foliar uptake.
- No mowing for 24 hours either side.
- Tank-mix friendly with Iron+, Liquid N, Charger, Humic+, NZLA PGR.

For routine monthly programme use: lower end of the rate range.
For pre-stress hardening or post-stress recovery: upper end of the rate range.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Amino+ + Iron+ = colour plus biology support
- Amino+ + Liquid N = nitrogen push plus biology support
- Amino+ + Humic+ = combined biostimulation (popular base programme tank-mix)
- Amino+ + NZLA PGR = growth regulation with stress tolerance support

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using about 100 to 120 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 40 applications, roughly two seasons of monthly use.

Don't double the rate for stronger effect. Amino acid uptake is rate-limited at the leaf and excess just runs off. Monthly applications at label rate outperform single high-rate applications.

# When should I apply Amino+?

Through the growing season at monthly intervals if running as a programme. Specific event-based use for stress hardening and recovery.

Programme approach: monthly through spring, summer and autumn (October to April for cool-season turf). Tank-mixed with other foliar applications to add biostimulation to the regular feed.

Event-based approach:
- 2 to 4 weeks before expected stress (heatwave, drought, renovation, scheduled chemical application).
- 1 to 7 days after stress event (recovery push).
- Pre-overseed and post-overseed for seedling support.
- Pre-event tank-mix with Iron+ and Liquid N for the lawn looking sharp.

Soil and air temps 14 °C or warmer for best response.

Avoid:
- Wet leaves (rain forecast within 6 hours).
- Hot stressed lawn over 28 °C with no irrigation.
- Active fungicide application that requires no biology interaction (read the fungicide label).

Apply with a calibrated sprayer to dry leaves. No watering in for 24 hours. Tank-mix friendly with most other foliar products.

For a typical home lawn programme, integrating Amino+ at monthly intervals tank-mixed with the regular nitrogen and iron applications adds biostimulation without an extra application trip across the lawn.

# NZLA Amino+

A premium liquid biostimulant built around plant-available amino acids, Vitamin B1, and beneficial biology. The active ingredients let the plant skip several energy-expensive metabolic steps it would normally do for itself, which matters most during stress recovery and critical growth windows.

## What it does

Plants normally take up nitrogen as nitrate or ammonium and use that nitrogen to build amino acids, the building blocks of protein. That process requires energy (ATP) and conditions favouring active metabolism. Under stress (cold, heat, drought, disease, recovery from wear), the plant's ability to build amino acids from raw nitrogen slows down or stops.

Amino+ supplies the amino acids directly. The plant can use them without having to make them, which short-cuts the metabolic load and gets the building blocks of growth into the plant faster. The energy that would have gone into amino acid biosynthesis can be redirected to other plant functions — root growth, photosynthesis, stress recovery.

## Why enzymatic digestion matters

Not all amino acid products are equal. Two main methods exist for extracting amino acids from plant protein, and they produce very different products.

**Acid hydrolysis** breaks plant protein apart with strong acid. It's cheap and produces a high-yield amino acid extract, but the harsh chemistry damages or destroys many of the amino acids in the process. The result is a high-percentage "amino acid" product where a lot of the amino acids aren't actually functional any more.

**Enzymatic digestion** uses biological enzymes to break the protein apart, gently and selectively. The amino acids come out intact and functional, in the same form the plant would build them. Amino+ uses enzymatic digestion specifically because the amino acids actually work in the plant.

Buying on the "amino acid percentage" alone can be misleading because the percentage doesn't tell you how many of those amino acids are still functional.

## The Vitamin B1 angle

Vitamin B1 (thiamine) is one of the lesser-known plant growth supports. It contributes to root development specifically, and it helps the plant recover from stress by supporting cellular repair processes. Its inclusion in Amino+ targets the recovery and establishment use cases.

## Rate and application

Foliar application: 200 mL per 100m² in 5 L water.

Soil application: 400 mL per 100m² in 4 to 8 L water, watered in immediately so the biology reaches the root zone before drying out.

## When to use

Post-stress recovery — after heat, drought, disease, or wear damage when the plant needs help building new growth.

Cool conditions where the plant's nitrogen metabolism is slow but you don't want to force soft growth with high-N feeding.

Establishment phase as part of seedling vigour support (alongside Liquid Starter).

Routine biological soil support every 4 to 6 weeks during the growing season.

Pre-stress preparation. Applying before known stress (a hot week forecast, a tournament, a wear event) gives the plant resources to handle the stress better.

## When not to use

As a primary nitrogen source. The 9% N analysis isn't designed to drive growth — it's a balanced delivery alongside the amino acids and biology.

In hot conditions where the biological component could be damaged. Apply in cool conditions or evening.

In equipment used for fungicides without thorough cleanout. Fungicide residue can damage the beneficial biology.

Mixed with high-rate fungicide tank mixes. Separate by 2 to 4 weeks for biology survival.

## Common mistakes

Treating it as a fertiliser substitute. Amino+ is a biostimulant supplement, not a replacement for All Seasons or All Seasons N+.

Skipping the water-in on soil applications. The biology dries out and dies on the surface.

Mixing with broad-spectrum fungicides. Defeats the biological purpose.

Buying on amino acid percentage alone. Enzymatic vs acid-hydrolysed makes a real difference.

## Best paired with

A solid base fertiliser programme.

NZLA Restore for additional biological soil support.

Liquid Boost or Charger for the nutrition side while Amino+ handles the amino acid and biological side.

Pre-stress application before known difficult periods.

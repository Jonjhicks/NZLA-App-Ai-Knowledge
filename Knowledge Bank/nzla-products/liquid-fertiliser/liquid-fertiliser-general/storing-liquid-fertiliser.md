# How should I store liquid fertilisers?

Cool, dry, out of direct sunlight, lid sealed tight. Most NZLA liquid products have a 2 to 3 year shelf life when stored properly, often longer.

Practical storage:
- Garage shelf in a temperate climate is fine. Not in the back corner where summer temps hit 40 °C.
- Don't store outside in full sun. The UV breaks down some components and the heat degrades biological products like Nurture, Amino+ and Root Health.
- Don't store at risk of freezing. Frozen liquid fertiliser thaws to a separated mess that doesn't re-mix properly.
- Keep lids tight. Open bottles let moisture and air in, which can degrade the product and grow algae in nutrient-rich formulations.

Shelf life varies by product type:
- Biological products (Nurture, Amino+ with live components): 12 to 18 months from opening, store cool.
- Kelp products (Root Health): 2 to 3 years if stored cool and out of sun.
- Synthetic foliar feeds (Charger, Liquid N, Liquid Boost, Iron+): 3 to 5 years stored properly.
- Once mixed in the tank, use within a few hours. Don't store mixed sprayer loads overnight.

If a product has separated, settled out, changed colour or smell from new, it's likely past its useful life. Mineral-based liquids that have started to crystallise out may still work if redissolved with warm water and agitation, but biological products that have separated have usually lost potency.

Keep products in their original labelled bottles. Decanting for convenience risks confusion later about what's what and at what concentration.

# What's a good foliar tank-mix combination?

A few proven combinations for typical situations.

Standard monthly foliar feed:
- Charger (slow-release foliar nitrogen base)
- Iron+ (colour)
- Amino+ (biostimulation and stress tolerance)
- Humic+ (soil chemistry and biology support)

One spray covers nitrogen, colour, biology, soil. Apply at lower-to-mid rates of each, monthly through the growing season.

Pre-event sharpening (1 week before the date):
- Liquid Boost (fast nitrogen push)
- Iron+ (strong colour response)
- Wetter if conditions are dry

Recovery push (post-disease, post-renovation):
- Liquid Boost or Liquid N
- Restore
- Amino+
- Iron+ (colour for the recovery)

Rust treatment:
- Fungus Pro (rust knock-down)
- Liquid N or Liquid Boost (nitrogen push for the recovery)
- Iron+ (colour)

Root push programme (pre-summer):
- Root Health (kelp for root development)
- Humic+ (soil chemistry support)
- Amino+ (biostimulation)
- Apply monthly through spring

Don't tank-mix:
- Two fungicides in the same FRAC group
- PGR with growth-pushing products (they work against each other)
- Strong herbicide with multiple foliar feeds (the lawn handles the herbicide better without additional load)
- Anything that isn't compatible (jar test if you're unsure)

Standard tank-mix sequence: water first, agitate, add products one at a time with agitation between each, never dump everything in at once.

# When should I apply Root Health?

Monthly through the growing season for the programme benefit, or as targeted applications around specific events.

Programme approach: monthly from October through April for cool-season turf. Tank-mixed with the regular foliar applications.

Specific situations:
- 4 to 6 weeks before expected summer drought (build root depth in advance).
- At new lawn establishment, starting 2 to 4 weeks post-germination.
- Post-renovation (after core aeration in particular, where you want roots filling the cores).
- After PGR application (the growth diversion to roots is amplified).
- On sandy soils with shallow rooting issues throughout the season.

Soil temps 14 °C or warmer for hormone response.

Apply to dry leaves with a calibrated sprayer. Light irrigation 4 to 6 hours after application is fine (the kelp components have some soil-active benefit). For a pure foliar approach, no irrigation for 24 hours.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Root Health + Humic+ = root development plus soil chemistry support
- Root Health + Amino+ = root development plus stress tolerance
- Root Health + Charger or Liquid Boost = root development with nitrogen for top growth
- Root Health + PGR = synergistic effect (PGR pushes energy to roots, kelp amplifies the response)

Don't tank-mix with fungicides without checking compatibility. Kelp extracts can react with some chemistries.

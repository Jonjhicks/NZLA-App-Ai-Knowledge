# What's the application rate for Root Health?

Check the current Root Health label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

For programme monthly use: lower end of the rate range.
For targeted establishment or pre-stress application: upper end.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m².
- Apply to dry leaves.
- Light irrigation 4 to 6 hours after application is acceptable.
- Tank-mix friendly with Humic+, Amino+, Iron+, Charger, Liquid N, NZLA PGR.

Don't double the rate hoping for faster root response. Plant hormone uptake is rate-limited at the leaf surface. Better to stick to label rates at monthly intervals than double-rate quarterly.

The kelp components are temperature-sensitive in storage. Keep the bottle out of direct sun and extreme heat. Quality kelp extracts last 2 to 3 years when stored properly. Older or heat-damaged product loses potency, the hormones break down.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using about 150 to 200 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 25 applications, roughly two seasons of monthly use.

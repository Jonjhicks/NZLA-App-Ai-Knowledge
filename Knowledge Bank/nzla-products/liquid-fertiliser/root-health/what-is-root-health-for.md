# What is NZLA Root Health used for?

Root Health is a kelp-based biostimulant focused on root system development. Kelp extracts contain naturally occurring plant hormones (cytokinins and auxins in particular) that drive root cell division and elongation.

What you'll see:
- Stronger and deeper root systems over weeks to months of application.
- Better drought tolerance (deeper roots reach deeper soil moisture).
- Better recovery from heat and stress.
- Improved establishment of new lawns and overseeded areas.
- Better tolerance to wear and traffic over time.

Where it fits:
- Pre-summer push to build root depth before drought stress.
- Establishment programme alongside Liquid Starter and NZLA Starter.
- Post-renovation, supporting root recovery in cored or scarified turf.
- High-input lawns under PGR programmes (PGR diverts energy from leaf growth to roots, Root Health amplifies the root development side).
- Sandy soils with shallow rooting issues.

The product is not a quick fix. Root development happens at the timescale of weeks and months, not days. A single Root Health application won't transform the lawn. A programme of applications across a season builds root depth and resilience that pays off in stress conditions.

Tank-mix friendly with most NZLA liquid products. Standard partner with Humic+ and Amino+ for a full biology-focused tank mix.

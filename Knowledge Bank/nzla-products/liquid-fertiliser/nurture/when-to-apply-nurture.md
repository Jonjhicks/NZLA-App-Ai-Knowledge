# When should I apply Nurture?

Monthly through the growing season for the programme value, or as targeted applications during establishment and recovery.

Programme approach: monthly from October through April for cool-season turf. Tank-mixed with other foliar products or applied standalone.

Targeted applications:
- New lawn establishment, starting at 4 to 6 weeks post-germination.
- Post-renovation (after scarifying, dethatching, core aeration).
- After fungicide or insecticide programmes that have hit soil biology.
- Pre-summer to build biology in advance of heat stress.

Soil temps 14 °C or warmer for the biology to establish. Cooler conditions slow microbial growth, the nutrient side still works but the biology takes longer to develop.

First two applications at the higher rate end to build the initial microbial population. Subsequent applications can drop to lower rate for maintenance.

Don't apply:
- Immediately before or after a fungicide application (the chemistry will affect the microbes).
- During drought stress without irrigation (microbes need moisture).
- To very hot dry surfaces (apply early morning or late afternoon).

Tank-mix friendly with Humic+, Amino+, Iron+, Liquid N. Don't tank-mix with strong chemical products like fungicide or herbicide (the biology won't survive the chemistry).

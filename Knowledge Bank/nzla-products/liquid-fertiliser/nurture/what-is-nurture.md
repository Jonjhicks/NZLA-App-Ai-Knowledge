# What is NZLA Nurture?

Nurture is a combined fertiliser and biological inoculant. The liquid contains organic-sourced NPK (10-1-11) plus a package of beneficial bacteria and fungi. The microbes establish in the soil, cycle nutrients, support root function, and contribute to long-term biology development.

The way it's different from straight liquid feeds:
- Charger, Liquid N, Liquid Boost are nutrient products. Nurture is nutrient plus biology.
- Humic+ supports biology indirectly through soil chemistry. Nurture introduces specific biology directly.
- Amino+ is a biostimulant. Nurture is biostimulation plus inoculation.

The trade-off: more expensive per litre than pure nitrogen products, and the response is slower because biology takes time to establish and build effect. The benefit comes over multiple applications across a season, not from a single hit.

Best fit for:
- New lawn establishment, where soil biology is starting from low base.
- Lawns recovering from heavy chemical input where biology has taken hits.
- Sandy or low-organic-matter soils with weak biological function.
- Lawn programmes oriented toward soil health and biology over time, not just immediate response.

Less suited to:
- Quick recovery push (Liquid Boost or Liquid N is faster).
- Pre-event sharpening (Iron+ is faster for colour).
- Single-application use (the value is in the programme, not the one-off).

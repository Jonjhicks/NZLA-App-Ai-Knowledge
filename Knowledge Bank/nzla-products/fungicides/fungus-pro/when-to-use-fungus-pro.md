# When should I use NZLA Fungus Pro?

Two main scenarios.

First, rust. Fungus Pro is the NZLA option for rust because Azoxy doesn't cover it. If your lawn's coming out summer with the orange dust on your shoes, Fungus Pro is what you want.

Second, as the rotation partner for Azoxy. Two consecutive Azoxy applications, then swap to Fungus Pro for the next spray to keep selection pressure off the strobilurin chemistry. That's the resistance management principle.

Fungus Pro is a contact protectant, not curative. It works best applied before disease establishes. Once you've got widespread visible infection, Fungus Pro will stop further spread but it won't reverse what's already there.

Residual is 10 to 14 days, shorter than Azoxy's 21 to 28 days. So if disease pressure is sustained (cool damp autumn weeks), expect to repeat the application more often.

For most disease situations in a home lawn programme, Azoxy is the first-line choice for its systemic activity and broader spectrum, and Fungus Pro comes in as the rust option and the rotation partner.

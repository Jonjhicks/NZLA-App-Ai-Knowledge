# Does Fungus Pro work on rust?

Yes. Fungus Pro is the NZLA option for rust. Thiram is broadly effective against rust diseases and Fungus Pro applied at label rate as a foliar spray knocks rust back well.

Best practice with rust is to combine chemistry with the cultural fix. Rust often shows up on stressed lawns lacking nitrogen, particularly through late summer and autumn. Spraying Fungus Pro takes the rust off but if the lawn stays nitrogen-starved, more rust shows up shortly after.

Standard rust response would be Fungus Pro on the foliage plus a small dose of nitrogen to push some growth into the lawn. Liquid N at a light rate, or a granular fertiliser if you're due for one. The combination of fungicide and feed usually clears the rust within a couple of weeks.

Azoxy doesn't cover rust, so if rust is your main disease issue, Fungus Pro is the product to keep on the shelf, not Azoxy.

Cultural prevention going forward: keep nitrogen levels adequate through late summer and autumn, mow at a reasonable height, water deeply rather than lightly.

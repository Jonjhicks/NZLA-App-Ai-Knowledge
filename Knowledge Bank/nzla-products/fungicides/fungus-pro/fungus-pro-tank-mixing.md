# Can I tank-mix Fungus Pro with other products?

Most NZLA foliar products, yes. Sensible partners include:
- Iron+ for colour while you're treating disease
- Amino+ and Humic+ for recovery support
- Liquid N at a low rate (particularly useful with rust)
- Wetter or Wetter 3W if you need help with coverage

Don't mix Fungus Pro with strongly alkaline products or copper-based products. Thiram is a sulphur-containing compound and reacts with copper.

Avoid mixing with another fungicide in the same tank, especially another contact product. The mode of action overlaps and you're not getting any benefit. Better to apply one, wait the residual period, then apply the other in rotation.

Tank-mix sequence: water in first, agitate, add Fungus Pro, agitate, then any partners. Jar test if you're trying a combination you haven't used before.

One practical note: thiram has a distinctive smell and can leave a slight yellow film on clothing if you spill it. Wear gloves and decent kit when mixing.

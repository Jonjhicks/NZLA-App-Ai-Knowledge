# Can I tank-mix NZLA Azoxy with other products?

Yes, with most NZLA foliar products. Azoxy plays nicely with:
- Iron+ (good combo when treating disease on a stressed lawn that needs colour back)
- Amino+, Humic+ (biostimulants supporting recovery)
- Liquid N at low rates
- Wetter or Wetter 3W if needed for coverage

Avoid mixing Azoxy with anything that's strongly acid or alkaline (most NZLA products are pH-balanced for the lawn so this isn't usually an issue). Don't mix with copper-based products, the combination can flocculate.

Standard tank-mix order applies: water first, agitate, add Azoxy, agitate, then any additional partners one at a time with agitation between each. Don't dump everything in at once.

If you're unsure about a specific combination, do a jar test first. Half-fill a jar with water, add the products at the rates you'd use in the tank, shake, let it sit for 30 minutes. If it stays uniform, you're fine. If it separates, curdles or settles, don't mix in the sprayer.

One thing not to mix: another systemic fungicide, particularly anything in FRAC Group 11 (other strobilurins). That's not resistance management, that's resistance promotion.

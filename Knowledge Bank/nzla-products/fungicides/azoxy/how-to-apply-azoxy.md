# How do I apply NZLA Azoxy?

23 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water. Foliar spray on dry foliage. Don't water in for 24 hours, this is a foliar disease product and it needs to stay on the leaf, not get washed into the soil.

A few practical details.

Get the water rate right. 4 to 8 L per 100 m² is the carrier window. Too low and coverage is patchy, too high and you're wasting time and water without improving the result. Aim for the middle (5 to 6 L) and calibrate the sprayer if you're not sure what it's actually putting down per minute.

Spray when the lawn is dry. Wet leaves dilute the product before it has time to absorb. A dewy morning lawn is too wet. Wait until it dries off.

Don't mow within 24 hours either side of application. Mowing right before removes leaf tissue, reducing the area available for the product to absorb. Mowing right after takes the freshly applied product off with the clippings.

Spray to wet but not run-off. If the product is dripping from the leaves onto the soil, you're putting too much down. Slow walking pace with a calibrated sprayer is the goal.

Maximum two consecutive applications before rotating to Fungus Pro or holding the spray until conditions reset.

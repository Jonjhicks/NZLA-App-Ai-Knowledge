# What does NZLA Wetter do?

Wetter is a soil surfactant. It breaks the surface tension on hydrophobic soil particles, letting water soak into the soil rather than beading off the surface.

Hydrophobic soil develops when organic matter coats soil particles with a waxy film that water doesn't penetrate. Common in sandy soils, soils that have dried out hard through summer, and soils with high organic matter that's gone through dry-wet cycles. Symptoms are dry patches that won't take water no matter how much you irrigate, hard crusty surface, water pooling and running off.

What you'll see after Wetter application:
- Water soaks into previously hydrophobic patches within 1 to 2 waterings.
- More even soil moisture across the lawn.
- Better fertiliser uptake (nutrients move with water into the soil).
- Reduced dry patch through summer.
- Sometimes a colour improvement as previously moisture-stressed turf rehydrates.

Where it fits:
- Summer dry patch treatment.
- Pre-summer preventative on sandy soils.
- Treatment for newly identified hydrophobic patches.
- Tank-mix with fertilisers to improve water and nutrient movement.

Wetter is standard residual (around 4 to 6 weeks per application). Wetter 3W is a longer-residual version (around 12 weeks). Choose based on application frequency you want to commit to.

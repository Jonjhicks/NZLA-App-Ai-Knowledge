# What's the application rate for Wetter?

Check the current Wetter label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water.

Practical points:
- Higher carrier water than typical foliar applications, the wetting agent is going into the soil, not staying on the leaf.
- Apply to a dry surface.
- Water in with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation within 24 hours of application.
- Tank-mix friendly with most NZLA products.

For an active dry patch (treatment): upper end of the rate range, single application, water in well.

For preventative programme use: lower end, monthly applications through the dry months.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Wetter + summer fertiliser application = helps the fertiliser move down through the soil
- Wetter + Humic+ = combined soil treatment
- Wetter + Aerate = penetrant plus surfactant for badly hydrophobic situations
- Wetter + Wetter 3W (single application) = short-term knockdown plus longer-term cover

Don't double the rate. Surfactant chemistry works at the rate it's designed for, doubling doesn't double the effect and can over-saturate the soil with surfactant residue.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 200 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 25 applications, roughly two seasons of monthly use through summer.

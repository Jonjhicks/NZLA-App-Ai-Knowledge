# When should I apply Wetter?

Through spring, summer and autumn (October to April for cool-season turf), at intervals of 4 to 6 weeks if running a programme.

Specific timing:
- Pre-summer preventative: October to November on lawns prone to dry patch.
- Active treatment: as soon as dry patches appear, repeat in 4 to 6 weeks if needed.
- Routine programme: monthly through the dry months.
- After dethatching or coring: helps water move into the freshly opened soil.

Soil temps don't matter much, Wetter works at any temperature. The reason to focus on the dry months is that hydrophobic conditions develop and matter most through summer. In winter, soils are wet enough that the surfactant isn't doing much work.

Apply to a dry lawn with a calibrated sprayer in 4 to 10 L water per 100 m². Water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation. The wetting agent needs to move into the top of the soil to start working on the hydrophobic particles.

Tank-mix friendly. Standard partner with summer fertiliser applications to help nutrients move down through the soil into the root zone.

Don't apply on saturated soil, the product won't establish in the soil if there's no room for it to move in. Wait for the soil to dry out, then apply.

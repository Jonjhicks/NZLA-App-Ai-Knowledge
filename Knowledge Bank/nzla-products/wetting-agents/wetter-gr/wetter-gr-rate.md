# What's the application rate for Wetter GR?

1.5 kg per 100 m² every 4 weeks during the growing season. Pack sizes are 4.5 kg (covers 300 m²) and 7.5 kg (covers 500 m²).

Practical application:
- Use a calibrated spreader. Hand broadcasting at this rate is hard to do evenly on anything over about 50 m².
- Cross-pattern application (half north-south, half east-west) for even coverage.
- No irrigation strictly required, but 10 to 15 mm of irrigation accelerates the release.
- Suits non-irrigated lawns where liquid applications can't be reliably watered in.

Compared to liquid Wetter (50 to 100 mL per 100 m²):
- Granular: 1.5 kg per 100 m² monthly, no watering required.
- Liquid: 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² monthly (or 80 to 150 mL of Wetter 3W every 12 weeks), requires watering in.

Don't combine with PGR or fungicide granular applications. Most other granular fertiliser applications can be co-applied if you can spread both products at their respective rates simultaneously.

For a 250 m² home lawn at 1.5 kg per 100 m², you're using 3.75 kg per application. A 7.5 kg bag covers two applications, roughly two months of cover. A 4.5 kg bag covers a bit over one application on a 250 m² lawn.

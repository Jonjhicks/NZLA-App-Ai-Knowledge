# What is Wetter GR used for?

Wetter GR is a granular wetting agent. Same surfactant chemistry as the three-way liquid Wetter 3W, delivered through a granular carrier that releases over weeks as the granule breaks down with moisture.

Why granular matters:
- No sprayer required. Apply with a standard granular spreader.
- No irrigation strictly required to activate the chemistry, suits lawns without irrigation systems.
- Larger areas covered faster than liquid applications.
- Steadier release of surfactant over time as the granule breaks down.

Where it fits:
- Non-irrigated lawns where you can't water in a liquid wetting agent reliably.
- Larger areas (lifestyle blocks, sports fields, large home lawns) where liquid application is impractical.
- Programmes where you want to combine wetting agent application with granular fertiliser application in a single pass.
- Lawns where the owner doesn't have or want a sprayer setup.

Compared to Wetter 3W (liquid):
- Same surfactant action.
- Granular release vs immediate liquid distribution.
- No watering-in requirement.
- Different application method (spreader vs sprayer).

For a typical home lawn with a working irrigation or hose setup, Wetter 3W in liquid form is cheaper per area. For lawns without irrigation or for owners who prefer granular application, Wetter GR is the suited choice.

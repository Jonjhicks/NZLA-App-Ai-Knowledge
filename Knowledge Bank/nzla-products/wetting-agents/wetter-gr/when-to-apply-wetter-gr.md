# When should I apply Wetter GR?

Every 4 weeks through the growing season. Spring through autumn (October to April for cool-season turf).

Apply at 1.5 kg per 100 m² with a calibrated spreader. Cross-pattern application (half north-south, half east-west) gives the most even coverage.

No irrigation strictly required to activate the product (the granule releases as natural moisture from dew and rainfall breaks it down). With irrigation, water in with 10 to 15 mm to accelerate the release.

Timing logic:
- Pre-summer: October to November to set up cover going into the dry season.
- Through summer: monthly maintenance applications.
- Post-renovation: helps water move into the freshly opened soil.

The 4-week interval is shorter than Wetter 3W's 12-week residual because the granular release is slower and the surfactant accumulates more gradually in the soil. Monthly applications maintain steady cover.

Tank-mix friendly with granular fertilisers if you can spread both at the same time. Co-applying with All Seasons or All Seasons N+ at their respective rates means one trip across the lawn covers both nutrient and surfactant needs.

Don't apply on saturated soil or during heavy rain. The granules need to settle into the canopy and start releasing into a soil that has room to absorb the chemistry.

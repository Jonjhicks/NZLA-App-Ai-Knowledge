# What's the application rate for Penetrate?

50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 10 L per 100 m² for even coverage.
- Apply before rainfall or water in with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation within 24 hours.
- Tank-mix friendly with NZLA Aerate on clay and silt soils, Humic+ for combined soil treatment, fungicides for disease-prone wet windows.
- Don't tank-mix with Wetter or Wetter 3W (opposite jobs).
- Don't double the rate to extend the interval.

For routine monthly use through wet months: lower end of the rate range.
For active treatment after heavy rain or before disease pressure builds: upper end.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 150 to 200 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 25 applications, roughly two to three seasons of monthly use through the wet months.

Older labels and communications may refer to this product as "Push" (renamed Penetrate in January 2025). Same active, same rates, same usage, only the name has changed.

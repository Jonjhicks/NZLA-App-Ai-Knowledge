# When should I apply Penetrate?

Through the wetter months, when canopy moisture lingers and surface dry-down is slow. Autumn through winter (April to August) is the standard window.

Specific timing:
- Pre-disease season (autumn): apply as autumn rains start, reduces canopy moisture going into fusarium patch and red thread season.
- After heavy rainfall events: accelerates surface dry-down.
- Monthly through winter on lawns prone to wet canopy and surface waterlogging.
- Pre-event on a wet lawn: if you need the surface firmer for an event, Penetrate can move water down so the surface dries out.

Apply before rainfall or water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation. The product needs to reach the soil profile to work, surface-only application won't do much.

Tank-mix friendly:
- Penetrate + Aerate on clay and silt soils
- Penetrate + Humic+ for combined soil treatment
- Penetrate + fungicide (Azoxy or Fungus Pro) for disease prevention through wet windows

Doesn't compete with summer dry patch use of Wetter. Different time of year, different problem. Run Wetter through summer for hydrophobic soil, Penetrate through autumn and winter for surface waterlogging.

Soil temps don't matter much, the chemistry works in cool and warm conditions. Application window is governed by when surface moisture is a problem, not by temperature.

# When should I apply Wetter 3W?

Once or twice through the dry season is usually enough.

Standard timing:
- Late October to early November (first application, sets up summer cover).
- February (optional second application if dry patch returns or extended hot dry summer is forecast).

The 12-week residual means a November application carries through January. A late January application carries through April if rains return. Two applications cover most NZ summers.

Apply to a dry surface with a calibrated sprayer at label rate in 4 to 10 L water per 100 m². Water in straight after with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation. The wetting agent needs to move into the top of the soil to establish.

Don't apply on saturated soil. Wait for the soil to dry out.

Avoid application during heatwaves above 30 °C. Apply in cooler morning or evening conditions, water in soon after.

Tank-mix friendly. Useful partners include Humic+ for combined soil treatment, Aerate for badly hydrophobic situations, summer fertiliser applications for combined nutrient and moisture movement.

For warm-season turf in northern NZ where the dry season runs longer, an additional March application can extend cover into autumn.

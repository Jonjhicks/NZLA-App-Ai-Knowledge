# What's the application rate for Wetter 3W?

Check the current Wetter 3W label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 80 to 150 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water.

The rate is higher than standard Wetter because the longer-residual chemistry needs more product established in the soil to last 12 weeks.

Practical points:
- Higher carrier water than typical foliar applications (4 to 10 L per 100 m²).
- Apply to a dry surface.
- Water in with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation within 24 hours.
- Don't apply on saturated soil.
- Tank-mix friendly with NZLA Humic+, Aerate, summer fertilisers.

Don't reduce the rate to stretch the bottle. Lower rates give shorter residual, and the cost saving is gone if you have to reapply twice as often. Either commit to label rate at 12-week intervals, or use standard Wetter at lower cost for the shorter-residual approach.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate (120 mL per 100 m²), you're using around 300 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 16 applications, roughly 4 years of one-application-per-year use on a single lawn.

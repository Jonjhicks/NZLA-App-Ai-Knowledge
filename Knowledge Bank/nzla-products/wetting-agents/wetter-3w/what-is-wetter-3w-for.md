# What is Wetter 3W used for?

Wetter 3W is a longer-residual soil surfactant. Same job as Wetter (breaks surface tension on hydrophobic soil, lets water soak in) but the effect lasts around 12 weeks per application instead of 4 to 6.

Where it fits:
- Set-and-forget summer cover. One application in October to November carries through the worst of summer dry patch season.
- Larger lawns or commercial situations where multiple monthly applications would be impractical.
- Lawns with persistent hydrophobic patches that don't resolve with the shorter-residual Wetter.
- Lawns where the owner won't or can't commit to monthly summer applications.

Compared to Wetter:
- Longer residual (12 weeks vs 4 to 6 weeks).
- Fewer applications per year (1 to 2 vs 5 to 6 through summer).
- Higher cost per litre.
- Total cost across a season is comparable, sometimes lower for Wetter 3W because of fewer applications.

For home lawn use, Wetter 3W is the simpler choice if you're not running other monthly applications. One application in late spring, one mid-summer if needed for problem areas, done for the season.

For lawns under a monthly foliar programme that tank-mixes wetting agent with regular nitrogen and iron applications, the shorter-residual Wetter integrates more cost-effectively.

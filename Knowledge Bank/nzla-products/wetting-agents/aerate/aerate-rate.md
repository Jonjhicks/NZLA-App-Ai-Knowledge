# What's the application rate for Aerate?

Check the current Aerate label for the exact rate. Working figure is around 50 to 100 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 10 L of water.

Practical points:
- Higher carrier water than typical foliar applications (4 to 10 L per 100 m²).
- Apply to a dry surface.
- Water in with 5 to 10 mm of irrigation within 24 hours.
- Don't apply on saturated soil.
- Tank-mix friendly with Wetter, Wetter 3W, Humic+, summer fertilisers.

For routine monthly programme use: lower end of the rate range.
For severe compaction or pre-aeration treatment: upper end.

Tank-mix combinations:
- Aerate + Wetter = combined hydrophobic and compaction treatment
- Aerate + Humic+ = soil chemistry and structure support
- Aerate + Liquid Boost or Charger = nutrient movement through tight soils

Don't double the rate to extend the interval. Surfactant and penetrant chemistry works at the rate it's designed for. Higher rates can over-saturate the soil with surfactant residue without improving the result.

For a 250 m² home lawn at mid-rate, you're using around 150 to 200 mL per application. A 5 L bottle covers around 25 applications, roughly two seasons of monthly use through the active programme.

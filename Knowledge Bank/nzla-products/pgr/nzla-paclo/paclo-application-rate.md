# What's the application rate for Paclo?

Check the Paclo label for the current rate range. Paclobutrazol is potent at low rates, so the rate window is narrow and over-application has long consequences.

Practical rate guide:
- Lower end of the rate range for poa suppression in a programme (multiple applications over a season at lower rates)
- Higher end for single-application growth regulation (longer effect, stronger expression)

Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m². Foliar plus some soil uptake. Light watering after application can help soil uptake on the poa suppression side, but excessive irrigation can over-distribute the chemistry. Read the label.

Don't double the rate to extend regulation. The chemistry will over-regulate, the lawn will stunt for months, and you've wasted product. Stick to label rates and use programme timing to extend the regulation period.

First-time user on a particular lawn: start at the lower end of the rate. Track results over 8 to 12 weeks. Scale up next application if needed.

Tank-mix friendly with NZLA Iron+, Amino+, Humic+, light Liquid N. Don't tank-mix with NZLA PGR (same pathway, additive effect can cause stunting).

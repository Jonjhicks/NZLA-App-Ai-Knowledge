# When should I apply NZLA PGR?

PGR works best on healthy actively-growing turf. The right window is the main growing season, spring through autumn (October to April for cool-season turf).

Soil temps 14 to 28 °C is the sweet spot. The chemistry is most active in this range. Below 14 °C the lawn isn't growing enough for PGR to be doing much. Above 28 °C the lawn is in heat stress and adding chemistry isn't going to help.

A few timing strategies:
- Pre-stress (hardening before summer). Apply in late spring as a single application. Lawn enters summer denser, more tillered, with better root depth.
- Mowing programme through summer. Apply every 4 to 6 weeks through the main growing season. Mowing frequency drops, lawn density improves, colour holds.
- Pre-renovation. Apply 3 to 4 weeks before a renovation to slow the lawn down going in, then drop it for the renovation period and let the lawn grow into the cores.

Don't apply to:
- Drought-stressed or heat-stressed turf
- Diseased turf
- New lawn under 8 weeks
- Lawn going into dormancy in late autumn

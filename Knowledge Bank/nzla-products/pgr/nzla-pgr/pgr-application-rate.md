# What's the application rate for NZLA PGR?

Check the current NZLA PGR label for the rate. Trinexapac rates for home lawn use sit around 8 to 15 mL per 100 m² depending on the desired regulation intensity and the turf species.

Practical points:
- Lower end of the rate range gives 4 weeks of regulation with mild rebound at the end.
- Upper end of the rate range gives 6 weeks of regulation with stronger rebound.
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m². Even coverage matters, patchy application = patchy regulation = uneven look.
- Apply to actively-growing healthy turf, dry leaves.
- Don't water in. PGR is foliar.

Tank-mix friendly with most NZLA foliar products. Combining with Iron+ at application gives a colour boost on top of the natural darker green from the regulation. Liquid N at low rate is fine. Don't tank-mix with a foliar fungicide and PGR at the same time unless you've checked compatibility.

A first-time application on a particular lawn, go to the lower end of the rate range. See how the lawn responds over 4 to 6 weeks, then dial up or down for the next application based on the result.

If you've gone too high or applied to a lawn that wasn't ready, the lawn can yellow or stall for a few weeks. Recovery is normal once growth resumes, but the lesson is to respect the rate range.

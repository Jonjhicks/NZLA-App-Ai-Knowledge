# What is Midnight Dark Ryegrass?

A premium perennial ryegrass cultivar selected for very dark green colour, fine leaf texture, and high turf density. Among the darkest perennial ryegrasses available in NZ.

Where it fits:
- Premium home lawns where appearance is the top priority.
- Sports field surfaces where density and colour matter.
- Overseeding existing lawns to improve colour and density over time.
- New lawn establishment for owners who want the best-looking option.

What you get:
- Darker base colour than standard ryegrass, requires less iron supplementation to look deep green.
- Finer leaf texture (closer to a fine fescue look while keeping ryegrass wear tolerance).
- Higher tiller density (more shoots per square metre, better sward coverage).
- Standard cool-season ryegrass behaviour (winter active, summer slower in heat-stressed regions).

Trade-offs:
- Cost more than standard ryegrass seed.
- Same wear tolerance as standard ryegrass (premium cultivars don't generally trade off wear).
- Same disease and drought susceptibility as ryegrass generally.

Sow at ryegrass establishment rates (around 25 to 35 g per square metre for a new lawn). Mix with Tall Fescue or Fine Fescue if you want a blended cool-season lawn.

For a top-end home lawn where you want the darkest cleanest look, MDR is one of the strongest cultivars in the NZLA range.

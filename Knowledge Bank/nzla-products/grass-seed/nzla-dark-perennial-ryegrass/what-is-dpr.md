# What is NZLA Dark Perennial Ryegrass?

A dark green perennial ryegrass cultivar with strong density and good warm-month activity. Sits between standard ryegrass and the premium Midnight cultivar on the colour and price scale.

What you get:
- Dark green base colour (darker than standard perennial ryegrass).
- Dense uniform sward.
- More active in warmer months than standard rye, which means better colour retention through summer in upper North Island conditions.
- Standard ryegrass wear tolerance and drought response.

Where it fits:
- Standard home lawns wanting a dark cultivar without the premium MDR cost.
- Overseeding existing lawns to improve colour over time.
- New lawn establishment where appearance matters but budget is mid-range.
- Upper North Island lawns where summer activity matters (DPR holds growth better than standard rye in heat).

Trade-offs:
- Not as dark as MDR.
- Standard leaf texture (not as fine as MDR).
- Same disease and pest susceptibility as ryegrass generally.

Sow at standard ryegrass establishment rates (25 to 35 g per square metre for new lawn, 15 to 20 g per square metre for overseeding).

DPR is the practical choice for most home lawn owners who want a noticeably dark green ryegrass without paying the MDR premium. Most lawns sown with DPR are darker than the neighbour's standard ryegrass without anything else changing.

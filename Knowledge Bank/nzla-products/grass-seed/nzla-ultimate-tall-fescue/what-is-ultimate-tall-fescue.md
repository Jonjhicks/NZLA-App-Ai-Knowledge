# What is NZLA Ultimate Tall Fescue?

A premium blend of dense fine-textured tall fescue cultivars. The high-end option in NZLA's tall fescue range.

What you get:
- Very dark green colour (dark for any tall fescue).
- Fine leaf texture (closer to ryegrass than typical coarse tall fescue).
- High density.
- Strong drought tolerance (tall fescue has deeper roots than ryegrass).
- Better heat tolerance than ryegrass.
- Standard tall fescue wear tolerance.

Where it fits:
- Premium home lawns where drought tolerance and dark colour both matter.
- Lawns in regions with hot dry summers (Canterbury, Hawke's Bay, inland Otago).
- New lawn establishment for owners wanting the best tall fescue option.
- Mixed cool-season lawn blends as the tall fescue component.

Trade-offs vs ryegrass:
- Slower to establish (germinates in 10 to 21 days vs 7 to 14 for ryegrass).
- Doesn't tiller as aggressively as ryegrass, so density takes longer to develop.
- More expensive per kg.

Trade-offs vs other tall fescues:
- Higher cost than standard tall fescue or Elfin.
- The colour and texture advantage justifies the cost on premium lawns.

Sow at 35 to 45 g per square metre for new lawn (higher than ryegrass because tall fescue seedlings establish more slowly). Overseed at 25 to 30 g per square metre.

For lawns in regions with summer drought stress where you want the best-looking drought-tolerant option, UTF is the strongest choice in the NZLA range.

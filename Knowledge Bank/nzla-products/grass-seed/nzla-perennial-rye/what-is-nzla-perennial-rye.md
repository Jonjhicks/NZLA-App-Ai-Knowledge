# What is NZLA Perennial Rye?

A standard quality perennial ryegrass for general home lawn establishment. The everyday option without premium-cultivar pricing.

What you get:
- Hard-wearing, suits family lawns.
- Winter active (keeps growing while some other ryegrasses go semi-dormant).
- Drought tolerant for a ryegrass (still less than tall fescue or kikuyu).
- Standard ryegrass green colour (not as dark as DPR or MDR).
- Quick establishment from seed (germinates in 7 to 14 days at optimal temps).
- Cost-effective for larger areas.

Where it fits:
- General home lawn establishment.
- Larger areas (lifestyle blocks, sports fields) where cost per area matters.
- Standard quality patch repair.
- Quick repairs to wear patches and high-use areas.

Trade-offs vs premium cultivars:
- Lighter colour than DPR or MDR.
- Standard leaf texture (broader than MDR or fine fescue).
- Doesn't have the density premium of higher-end cultivars.

For most NZ home lawns being established or repaired by someone who wants a solid functional lawn without paying for premium aesthetics, NZLA Perennial Rye is the right choice. The price advantage at scale (overseeding a large lawn, sowing a lifestyle block) is real.

Mix with tall fescue or fine fescue for blended cool-season lawns. Mix with DPR or MDR for partial colour upgrade at lower cost than full premium cultivar sowing.

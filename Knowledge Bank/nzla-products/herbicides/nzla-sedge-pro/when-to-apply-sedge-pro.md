# When should I apply NZLA Sedge Pro?

Sedge Pro is for sedge control when Halo isn't getting the result, or where you've got a heavy persistent sedge problem from the outset. Apply when sedges are actively growing, spring through summer (October to March).

Soil temps 18 °C or warmer. Sedges grow in warm conditions, the herbicide works best when they're pushing.

Sedges well-watered. Drought-stressed sedges don't take up chemistry effectively. If you've had a dry spell, water the lawn deeply a day or two before spraying.

No rain forecast for 6 to 8 hours after application.

Expect 3 to 4 weeks for full visible knockdown. Sedges don't drop overnight, and the underground tubers (the bit that lets nutgrass keep coming back) need time to be affected by the systemic chemistry.

Two applications 4 to 6 weeks apart is the typical programme for established nutgrass infestations. One spring application followed by a late summer follow-up if needed.

Established lawn only, check the label for registered turfgrass species.

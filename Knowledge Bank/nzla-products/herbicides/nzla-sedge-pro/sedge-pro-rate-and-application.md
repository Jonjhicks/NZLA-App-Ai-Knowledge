# What's the rate and application for Sedge Pro?

Check the current Sedge Pro label for the exact rate. Sulfonylurea-based sedge chemistry works at low rates per area, so getting the rate right matters more than with bigger-rate products.

Practical points:
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m². Even coverage is essential, patchy spray = patchy result.
- Add a wetting agent (NZLA Wetter at label rate). The chemistry benefits from improved leaf penetration on the waxy sedge leaves.
- Spray on dry foliage with no mowing for 3 days before or after.
- No rain or irrigation for 6 to 8 hours after application.
- Allow 3 to 4 weeks for visible knockdown.
- Heavy infestations: second application 4 to 6 weeks after the first.

Don't double the rate hoping for faster results. Sulfonylurea over-rates damage turfgrass without improving sedge control. Stick to label.

Don't tank-mix with hormone broadleaf herbicides at full rates of each, the combined chemistry load can stress the lawn. If you need both sedge and broadleaf control, run separate applications 10 to 14 days apart, or use Halo which covers both.

# What's the application rate for Etho?

Check the current Etho label for the exact rate. Working figure for a single application is around 100 to 200 mL per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

Multiple lower-rate applications usually outperform a single higher-rate application:
- 100 mL per 100 m² in April, repeat at 100 mL in May, repeat at 100 mL in June.
- Better poa knock-back with less risk of damaging the ryegrass than 300 mL in a single hit.

Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m². Foliar spray, doesn't need to be watered in.

Practical points:
- Don't mow for 3 days either side of application.
- Apply when the lawn is dry, soil is moist but not waterlogged.
- Don't apply when grass is heat-stressed or drought-stressed.
- Wind drift onto fine fescue, browntop, couch or kikuyu lawns will damage them, even small amounts.

Expect a brief yellowing or paling of the lawn for a week or two after application. That's normal, not damage. The lawn greens back up as it metabolises the chemistry.

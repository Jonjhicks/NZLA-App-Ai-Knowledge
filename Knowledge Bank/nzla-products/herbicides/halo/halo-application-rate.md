# What's the application rate for Halo?

Check the Halo label for the current rate. Halosulfuron rates are relatively low because the chemistry is potent at small doses. Working figure for typical sedge control is around 1 to 2 g per 100 m² in 4 to 8 L of water.

Practical points:
- Add a wetting agent (NZLA Wetter at label rate) if not already in the mix. The sulfonylurea chemistry benefits from improved leaf coverage and penetration.
- Carrier water 4 to 8 L per 100 m². Not enough water = patchy coverage = patchy control.
- Spray on a dry lawn that's not been mown for 3 days. Don't mow for 3 days after either.
- No rainfall or irrigation for 6 to 8 hours after application.
- Allow 3 to 4 weeks for visible knockdown. Don't reapply too early thinking the first hit failed.

If the result is partial after 4 weeks, a second application can be made at the same rate. Don't double the rate hoping for faster results, you risk turf damage without improving the sedge kill.

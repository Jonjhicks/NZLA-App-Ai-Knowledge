# What is NZLA Moss used for?

A contact moss killer for fine turf, lichen, liverwort and algae. Benzalkonium chloride at 500 g/L, kills moss in 5 to 15 minutes after contact.

What it does:
- Kills moss, lichen, liverwort and algae on contact.
- Fast knockdown (visible browning within 30 minutes).
- Doesn't move into the soil or the turf grass.
- Doesn't fix the underlying conditions that cause moss (shade, wet, low fertility, compaction). Those need separate attention.

Where it fits:
- Fine turf where moss has established (cotula, browntop, fine fescue greens).
- Damp shaded areas of home lawns where moss is taking over.
- Pre-renovation treatment to kill moss before scarifying or dethatching.
- Spot treatment for visible moss patches.

The product is registered for fine turf and cotula. Read the label for current registration and turf restrictions.

Doesn't solve the moss problem long-term on its own. Moss returns within weeks or months unless the underlying conditions change. Combine NZLA Moss with cultural correction (shade reduction, better drainage, improved fertility, aeration) for lasting results.

For pre-renovation use, killing the moss with NZLA Moss before mechanical scarifying makes the dethatching cleaner and easier, the dead moss comes out rather than tearing live moss apart.

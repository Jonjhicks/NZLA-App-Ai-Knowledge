# What is foliar fertilising and when do I use it?

Foliar means applying liquid fertiliser as a spray that the leaf absorbs directly, rather than via the roots from a granular product in the soil.

Foliar response is faster than granular. The leaf takes the nutrient up directly — colour and growth response within 3 to 5 days, compared to 7 to 14 days for granular as the soil microbes break it down and the roots take it up.

Four main situations where foliar makes sense:

**Cool conditions.** When soil temperatures drop below about 10°C, granular fertiliser response slows right down because the soil biology and root activity have slowed. Foliar uptake does not depend on the soil — the leaf takes it up directly — so a foliar programme through winter keeps the lawn fed when granular cannot.

**Top-up between granular applications.** The granular base feeds every 6 to 8 weeks. Foliar fills the gap between with a more responsive feed.

**Colour without growth.** Iron+ (pure iron, no nitrogen) lifts colour without forcing growth. Useful heading into cold conditions or pre-event when you want the appearance without the soft growth.

**Establishment and recovery.** Liquid Starter (8-10-5 with kelp) supports new lawns through the establishment window. Amino+ and Restore for post-stress recovery.

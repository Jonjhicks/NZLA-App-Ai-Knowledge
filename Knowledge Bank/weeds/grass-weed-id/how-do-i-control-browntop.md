# How do I control browntop in a ryegrass lawn?

NZLA Meso (mesotrione) is the chemical option. Cultural management and patience close out the long-term picture.

Chemistry programme:

NZLA Meso (mesotrione 480 g/L) — two applications 14 days apart. The chemistry has selectivity that damages browntop while leaving perennial ryegrass and tall fescue alone.

Apply when browntop is actively growing in mild conditions. Spring (October-November) or autumn (March-April).

EPA cap: maximum 2 applications per area per 12 months. The two-application browntop programme uses the entire annual allowance in one go.

Expectation:

Single 2-application programme controls 30 to 70% of browntop depending on rate, conditions and infestation severity. Cambridge University research on creeping bentgrass control (related species, similar response) shows wide variability.

For lawns with heavy browntop infestation, multi-year programmes are realistic. Each year's allowed application reduces the population further. Complete eradication may take 2 to 3 years.

For lawns with light browntop infestation (scattered small patches):

Spot treatment with glyphosate plus NZLA Sticker on individual patches may be more efficient than full-lawn mesotrione. Kills the browntop and a small surrounding area; reseed with NZLA Perennial Rye.

Cultural management:

A dense well-fed ryegrass lawn outcompetes browntop establishment. Steady fertility programme (All Seasons every 6 to 8 weeks through growing season) supports the ryegrass against new browntop encroachment.

Mowing height at the higher end of ryegrass range (30 to 50 mm) gives the ryegrass canopy advantage over browntop, which prefers tighter mowing.

After mesotrione treatment, the killed browntop areas show as bare or thin patches for 4 to 8 weeks. NZLA Starter granular and overseeding with NZLA Perennial Rye fills the patches.

Source of new browntop:

Browntop often enters lawns via:
- Wind-borne seed from neighbouring pastures or wild areas
- Contaminated grass seed (cheap seed mixes sometimes contain browntop)
- Lawn renovation work where browntop seed has dormant on adjacent areas

Reducing the source where possible (use only certified clean seed for renovations, fence-line management where seed is blowing in) reduces ongoing pressure.

For premium showcase lawns:

Multi-year programme combining Meso, cultural management, and source reduction. Achievable to clear browntop in 2 to 3 years on most home lawns.

For routine home lawns:

Annual Meso programme combined with cultural improvement. Browntop reduced over time even if not fully eliminated.

Note the chemistry limitation:

Once mesotrione's 2 annual applications are used, no further chemical browntop control until the 12-month cycle resets. Plan applications strategically.

# Where can I buy NZLA products?

Three options.

**NZLA website.** newzealandlawnaddicts.com — the full product range, including specialty products that retail stockists may not carry. Online ordering with delivery anywhere in NZ. Best option for the complete range, programme builders, and bulk or trade pricing.

**Click & Collect at Rolleston, Canterbury.** Order online from the NZLA website, collect from the Rolleston warehouse within an hour during business hours. Useful when you need product today and can drive in. Most efficient option for Canterbury customers.

**Independent stockists.** A network of garden centres, hardware stores and rural supply outlets across NZ stock NZLA products. Particularly useful if you want to see the product, ask local advice, or need something on the day. The product range varies by stockist — the larger stockists carry most of the granular and liquid fertiliser range, plus the common herbicides and fungicides; smaller stockists may stock just the popular lines.

The website has a stockist locator that shows the nearest outlets to your address.

Some practical notes:

Specialty products (NZLA Moss, Sedge Pro, specific PGRs) are more reliably available through the website than from retail stockists.

Bulk pack sizes (20 kg granular bags, larger liquid sizes) are typically only on the website.

Pricing varies. Stockists set their own retail prices; the website prices are direct from NZLA. Trade and bulk pricing is available through the website for orders meeting the volume threshold.

For NZ-specific advice on which stockists carry which products, check the website stockist locator or contact NZLA directly through the website contact form.

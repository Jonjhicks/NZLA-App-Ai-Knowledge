# Where do I order NZLA products?

Direct through the NZ Lawn Addicts website at newzealandlawnaddicts.com or through the NZLA app, which mirrors the same range and pricing. Both run on the same account, so anything you've bought through one shows up on the other.

The full product range is on the website. Every fertiliser, herbicide, fungicide, PGR, wetting agent, grass seed and accessory is there with full descriptions, label rates and use cases. The app makes it easier on a phone but the catalogue is identical.

A handful of NZLA products are also stocked through some garden centres and lawn care suppliers around NZ. The range available through stockists is more limited than the full direct-order catalogue. If you can't find a specific product locally, the website will have it.

For technical advice on what to order, the forum on the app or website is a fast way to get input from other users and Jon. For one-on-one questions you can email or contact NZLA directly through the support channels listed on the website.

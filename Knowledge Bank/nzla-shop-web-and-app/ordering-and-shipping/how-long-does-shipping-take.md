# How long does NZLA shipping take?

Standard delivery to most NZ urban addresses is 2 to 4 working days once the order's dispatched. Orders placed before the cut-off go out the same day or next working day depending on the day of the week.

Rural delivery typically adds another working day or two on top of standard delivery.

South Island delivery from the North Island distribution can take a day longer than North Island to North Island, simply because of the Cook Strait leg.

During the peak spring season (September into November) and around public holidays, courier networks slow down and delivery times can stretch a bit. Worth ordering a week earlier than you'd otherwise plan for spring projects.

For specific tracking and current cut-off times, the website checkout shows what's available for your address and the order confirmation email has tracking once the courier picks it up.

If something doesn't turn up when expected, contact NZLA support and they'll chase the courier.

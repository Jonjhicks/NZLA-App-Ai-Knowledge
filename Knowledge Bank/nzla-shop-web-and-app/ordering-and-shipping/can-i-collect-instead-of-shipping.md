# Can I collect my order instead of paying shipping?

Click and collect is available at the NZLA pickup point. Order online or through the app, choose click and collect at checkout, and you'll get a notification when the order's ready to grab.

That's the main saving point if you're near the pickup location. Worth it on heavier orders (bags of granular fertiliser, multiple bottles) where shipping adds up.

For specific pickup locations and current click-and-collect availability, check the website at checkout, the available options depend on the address you're collecting to.

Some NZLA stockists around NZ also carry parts of the range. If you've got a stockist nearby, picking up directly from them avoids shipping but you're limited to whatever stock they happen to carry.

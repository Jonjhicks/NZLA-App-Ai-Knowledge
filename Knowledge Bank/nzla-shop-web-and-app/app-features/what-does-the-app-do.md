# What does the NZLA app do?

Three main things, plus a few extras.

Shop. The full NZLA product range with the same pricing as the website. Browse, search, order, track delivery. Saved payment methods make repeat ordering quick.

Forum. The community part of the app. Post questions about your lawn with photos, get answers from Jon and other regular users, scroll through threads from other people dealing with the same issues. Honestly the best part of the app for most users.

Journal. Track what you've done on your lawn over time. Log mowing, fertilising, herbicide applications, weather, photos. Useful for spotting patterns over a season (when did the rust show up last year, how long since the last fertiliser, etc.) and for going back and seeing what was working when the lawn looked best.

Beyond that, there's the AI Lawn Expert in the app for technical questions, push notifications for new content or seasonal reminders, and direct access to NZLA support.

The website covers the shop and account side. The app adds the forum, journal and Lawn Expert on top.

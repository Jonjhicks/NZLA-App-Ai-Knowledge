# Customer Support

## How to contact NZLA

The contact form on the website at newzealandlawnaddicts.com is the main channel. Messages go directly to Jon's phone and are responded to within 24 hours for order-related questions.

There is no public phone number. NZLA is a small two-person team running an online retail and wholesale operation. When the number was listed, call volume from general lawn advice questions made it unworkable at 20,000+ customers. It's been removed deliberately.

**For order issues** (wrong item, missing item, courier problem, account query): use the contact form. This is what it's for.

**For lawn advice and product questions:** the app forum or the NZLA Facebook group are the right places. The contact form won't get a response for general lawn advice questions.

## Lawn advice

NZLA doesn't offer one-on-one phone or email advice consultations. The team is focused on product supply, not advisory services.

For lawn advice, two options:

**Facebook group** (free): facebook.com/groups/nzlawnaddicts. 20,000+ members including 400+ lawn industry professionals. Good for fast questions and photo ID of  or . Active moderation.

**NZLA App** ($40/year): The app forum is where Jon spends most of his time for technical questions. Better for detailed programme questions, back-and-forth discussion, and anything requiring focused expert input. Also includes the lawn journal and AI Lawn Expert.

If a situation genuinely needs professional eyes (structural drainage-and-soil-moisture issues, unusual commercial turf problem), the recommendation is to contact a local turf professional.

## Warehouse and location

The NZLA warehouse is in iZone, Rolleston (approximately 20 minutes from Christchurch). It is not open to the public. The site is a registered chemical handling facility — no public access under insurance and regulatory requirements.

Click and Collect orders are collected via a contactless lockbox. There is no counter, no staff on-site for retail, and no walk-in purchasing.

Business hours are 8am to 5pm Monday to Friday for support queries. Click and Collect collection is 24/7.

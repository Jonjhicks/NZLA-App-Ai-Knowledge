# How do I contact NZLA support?

Email is the main channel for direct support. Contact details are on the website at newzealandlawnaddicts.com under the contact or help pages.

For most lawn questions, the forum in the app is faster. Post a question with a photo, you'll usually have responses within a day from Jon and other regular users. Useful for product recommendations, identifying weeds or diseases, timing questions, and most technical advice.

For order-related issues (missing items, courier problems, account questions, refunds), email direct so it goes to the right person.

If you've got a question about whether a specific product is suitable for your lawn, you can ask through the forum or the AI Lawn Expert in the app. The AI handles a lot of the standard questions about timing, rates and compatibility quickly. For trickier situations or anything where you want a human eye on it, the forum or email are the way to go.

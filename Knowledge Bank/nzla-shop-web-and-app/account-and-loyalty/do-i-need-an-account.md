# Do I need an account to order from NZLA?

You can guest checkout for a single order without setting one up. For everything else, having an account makes things easier.

Saved addresses, order history, faster repeat ordering, access to the app forum and journal features, and any loyalty or repeat-customer benefits all sit behind the account. Setup is just an email and a password, takes under a minute.

The same account works across the website and the app, so anything you set up on one is there on the other.

If you've ordered before and forgotten your password, the password reset on the login page does the job. If you've got duplicate accounts from years of ordering, NZLA support can merge them so order history is in one place.

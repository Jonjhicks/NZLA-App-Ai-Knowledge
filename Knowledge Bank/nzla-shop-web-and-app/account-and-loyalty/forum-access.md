# How do I get access to the NZLA forum?

The forum is built into the NZLA app. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play, log in with your NZLA account (or set one up if you don't have one), and the forum is there in the main menu.

Posting and replying is open to all account holders. You can post photos of your lawn, ask questions, share what's working or not working, and read through threads from other users dealing with the same issues you are.

Jon and the NZLA team check the forum regularly and chime in on technical questions, particularly around product use and seasonal timing. Other regular users with strong lawn knowledge contribute too, so you often get multiple perspectives on a question within a day or two.

If you're stuck on a specific issue (a weed you can't identify, a disease showing up, a fertiliser timing question), the forum is the fastest way to get a useful answer rather than emailing or sitting on chat.

# How do I order from NZLA?

The main route is the NZLA website at newzealandlawnaddicts.com. Browse the full product range, add what you need to the cart, check out, and we'll dispatch from the warehouse.

Delivery is typically 2 to 3 business days for North Island addresses and 3 to 5 days for the South Island and rural delivery. Larger orders (granular fertiliser in 10 kg or 20 kg bags) may take an extra day for freight scheduling.

If you're not sure what to order, the website has a programme builder that walks through which products suit your lawn type, region and current season. Useful for new customers building a complete maintenance programme rather than picking individual products one at a time.

Bulk and trade pricing is available for larger orders — contact the team through the website if you're after volume pricing for a sports field, golf course, or commercial maintenance contract.

For repeat orders, the website remembers your previous purchases. Reorder takes a couple of minutes.

If you need product fast and can collect rather than wait for shipping, Click & Collect is available from the Rolleston (Canterbury) warehouse. Order online, then collect in person within an hour during business hours.

NZLA products are also stocked through a network of independent garden centres, hardware stores and rural supply outlets — particularly useful if you want to see and handle the product before buying, or you need something today. The website has a stockist locator if you want to find your nearest one.

For technical questions about which product suits your situation, the team is reachable through the website contact form or the NZLA Facebook group.

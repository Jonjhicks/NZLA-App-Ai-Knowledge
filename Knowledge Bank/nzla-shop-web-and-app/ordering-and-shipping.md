# Ordering and Shipping

## How to order

Order direct through the NZLA website at newzealandlawnaddicts.com, or through the NZLA app. Both carry the full product range at the same prices and run off the same account, so your order history shows up on either one.

Guest checkout is available for one-off orders. An account saves your address, payment method and order history for faster repeat ordering.

The full catalogue is only on the website and app. A smaller selection is stocked by independent garden centres and rural supply stores around NZ. If you can't find a specific product locally, the website will have it.

## Shipping

Castle Parcels handles deliveries.

**South Island:** Overnight for orders placed before 2pm Monday to Thursday.

**North Island:** 2 business days.

**Rural or non-residential:** Add 1 to 2 extra business days.

Orders placed before 2pm Monday to Friday dispatch the same day. Weekend orders leave the warehouse Monday morning.

Pickup day is not counted in the delivery estimate. Timeframes are guidance, not guarantees.

Shipping cost is calculated at checkout. Free shipping on all orders over $400.

Once dispatched, tracking details are emailed to the address used at checkout. Check spam if it doesn't show. If tracking doesn't arrive within 24 hours, the most common cause is a typo in the email address at checkout — contact NZLA via the website contact form to fix it.

For courier queries or to chase a parcel, Castle Parcels direct: customer.services@castleparcels.co.nz or 03 345 6840. If an order hasn't moved in 7 or more business days from dispatch, contact NZLA.

## Click and Collect

Click and Collect is available from the NZLA warehouse at Rolleston, iZone (approximately 20 minutes from Christchurch city). The warehouse is not a retail store and there is no counter staff — collection is fully contactless via a lockbox, available 24/7.

Order through the website and select Click and Collect at checkout. An email with the lockbox code and collection address is sent once the order is packed. Orders placed before 2pm Monday to Friday are usually ready within an hour, though this may be longer if staff are out doing runs.

It's the best option for Canterbury customers who need product the same day, or for heavy orders where shipping cost adds up.

## Stockists

NZLA products are stocked at garden centres, hardware stores and rural supply outlets across NZ. The range varies by stockist — most carry the popular granular and liquid fertilisers plus the common herbicides and fungicides. Specialty products (NZLA Moss, Sedge Pro, specific PGRs) are more reliably available through the website. Bulk pack sizes are website-only.

Stockist pricing is set independently. The website stockist locator shows the nearest outlets to your address.

## Adding to or cancelling an order

Adding to an existing order is generally not possible. Once packed, orders are on pallets ready for the courier run and can't be accessed. This applies to weekend orders too.

Cancellation is not possible once an order is packed. Orders placed in error or changed-mind orders are not eligible for refund.

## Returns and issues

**Change of mind or incorrect order placed:** Not accepted. Biological products (grass seed, liquid microbe-packed fertilisers) leave NZLA in controlled conditions and can't be resold once returned, as storage conditions in transit are unknown. The NZLA Buy and Sell Facebook group (facebook.com/groups/nzlabuysellgroup) is an active nationwide marketplace for this.

**Wrong item received:** Contact via the website contact form within 2 weeks of receiving the parcel. NZLA will send a return courier label; once received, the correct item is sent or a refund issued. The packing station is monitored by CCTV, footage kept on a 3-week loop. Claims after 3 weeks can't be verified.

**Missing items:** Same process — contact within 2 weeks. The 3-week CCTV loop applies for verification.

**Damaged in transit:** Contact via the website contact form.

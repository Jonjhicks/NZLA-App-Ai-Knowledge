## General & Warehouse Information

### Why no public phone number?
NZLA is primarily an online retailer and wholesale supplier, not an advisory service. When the number was listed publicly, high call volume (mostly lawn advice questions already answered on the website, labels, Facebook group, and App) made it unmanageable with a 20,000+ customer base. No longer published.

**For order enquiries:** Contact form below — messages go to Jonny's phone, response within 24 hours. General lawn advice enquiries via contact form are not responded to.

### Where is NZLA located?
Distribution warehouse in iZone, Rolleston (approx. 20 minutes from Christchurch). **Not open to the public.** No retail shop. No staff on-site for lawn advice. Registered chemical handling site — public access not permitted under insurance and WorkSafe/EPA regulations for chemical handling and storage.

### Can I collect in person?
Yes — **Click & Collect only**, fully contactless. Orders must be placed through the website. Once ready, customer receives an email with:
- Click & Collect lockbox code
- Collection address
- Relevant details

Collection is available 24/7. No staff on-site. Orders packed each morning from 9am, with additional runs during the day when possible.

**Timing:** Orders placed before 2pm Mon–Fri ready within approximately one hour (may be longer if team is out). Orders packed over the weekend too.

If email not received within 24 business hours, email address was likely entered incorrectly at checkout — contact via form.

### Business hours
8am to 5pm, Monday to Friday.

## Shipping & Orders

### Courier
Castle Parcels. NZLA uses them for volume and weight consistency — tried others, found Castle Parcels most competitive and consistent.

### Shipping costs
Calculated at checkout. **Free shipping on all orders over $400.**

### Delivery timeframes
- **South Island:** Overnight via NZ Couriers for orders placed before 2pm Mon–Thu
- **North Island:** 2 business days via Castle Parcels
- **Rural/non-residential:** Add 1–2 extra business days
- **Note:** Pickup day not counted. Timeframes are indications only — not guaranteed. Business days only (Mon–Fri, excluding public holidays).
- **Example:** 2-day service leaving Monday = targeted delivery Wednesday

### Dispatch timing
Orders placed before 2pm Mon–Fri dispatched same day. Weekend orders leave warehouse on Monday.

### Adding to an order
Generally not possible — parcels loaded onto pallets ready for dispatch. Weekend orders are also packed over the weekend, so this applies regardless.

### Cancelling an order
Not possible once packed. Parcel is on a pallet and unloading to find it isn't an option. **No refunds for orders placed in error or change of mind.**

### Tracking
Tracking details automatically emailed to address used at checkout. Check spam/junk first. If missing, email address was likely entered incorrectly — contact via form.

For updates, contact courier directly:
- **Castle Parcels email:** customer.services@castleparcels.co.nz
- **Castle Parcels phone:** 03 345 6840

If more than 7 business days since dispatch with no movement, use contact form.

## Returns & Issues

### Change of mind / incorrect order
**No refunds or exchanges for change of mind or incorrect orders.**

Reason: Products (grass seed, fertiliser, microbe-packed liquids) are "alive." Once they leave NZLA, storage conditions can't be controlled — couriers may leave in sun, stored in hot sheds. Cannot resell product without knowing what conditions it has been through.

**Alternative:** NZLA Buy & Sell Facebook group — www.facebook.com/groups/nzlabuysellgroup/ — large active community nationwide, most listings sell quickly.

### Damaged in transit
Contact via contact form.

### Wrong item received
Contact within **2 weeks** of receiving parcel. After 2 weeks, NZLA may not be able to correct errors. Use contact form. NZLA will email courier label for return; once received, will send correct item or issue refund.

**Packing station CCTV:** Monitored by video camera for quality control. Footage kept on **3-week loop** (automatically overwrites). Claims after 3 weeks cannot be verified and are not eligible for refund or exchange.

### Missing items
Contact within **2 weeks** of receiving order. CCTV footage (3-week loop) used to verify. Claims outside 3 weeks cannot be investigated.

## Lawn Advice

### Why no personal lawn advice?
Two-person team. Deliberate choice to focus on sourcing professional turf products rather than running advisory service. No one-on-one phone or email consultations.

**Where to get advice:**
1. **Facebook group** — 20,000+ members including 400+ lawn industry professionals. Free. Fast. Great for quick questions and photos.
2. **NZLA App** — $40/12 months. Lawn journal, mowing/application tracking tools, in-app forum. Higher quality discussion. **Jonny spends most of his time here answering questions directly.** Best place for direct access to his knowledge.

Jonny has shifted his primary focus to the NZLA Lawn Forum (part of the App). The Facebook group is better for open, fast-moving community discussion; the App is better for focused expert input.

**Contact form policy:** Cannot respond to lawn care questions, product recommendations, or application advice through the contact form. If unable to use the resources provided, recommendation is to contact a local turf professional.

# NZLA Website — Application Guide
**Source:** https://www.newzealandlawnaddicts.com/application-guide/
**Last retrieved:** 2026-06-06
**Purpose:** Authoritative reference for when to apply NZLA products and in what sequence. KB entries must be consistent with this schedule.

**Note:** The application guide page uses JS-rendered accordion panels for descriptions. The product lists per week are captured below but individual application instructions (rates, descriptions) were not rendered. Refer to product labels and website-content-pull.md for rates.

---

## General Application Rules (from page header)

- All rates based on **100m²**
- All products should be applied a minimum of **6 hours after mowing**
- Apply when temperatures **do not exceed 25°C**
- **Avoid mowing for 24 hours after application**
- If products don't state "irrigate after application" on the label or below, they are foliar feeds — **leave on the plant, apply to dry foliage**

## Key Observations for KB Use

- **Wetter 3W** appears at the start of warmer months (Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr, Oct, Nov, Dec) — roughly monthly during active growing season, less in winter
- **Grub+** appears twice: March Week 2 and October Week 1/2 — aligns with autumn and spring treatment windows
- **All Seasons (standard)** appears March Week 4 and May Week 1 — autumn applications
- **All Seasons N+** appears September Week 1, October Week 3, November Week 4 — spring/summer applications
- **Charger** appears April through August — predominantly cool/slow-growing period
- **Liquid Starter** appears July Week 1 only — mid-winter root establishment focus
- **Amino** appears Jan Week 2, Feb Week 2, Mar Week 3, Nov Week 2, Dec Week 2 — active season amino applications
- **Restore** is consistent throughout the year (appears most months)
- **Root Health** is consistent throughout the year
- **Iron+** appears predominantly summer (Jan, Feb) and winter/transitional (Jun, Jul, Aug, Dec)
- **Liquid N** appears summer only (Jan Week 3, Dec Week 1, Dec Week 4)
- **Liquid Boost** appears autumn through spring (Mar–May, Sep–Nov) — transitional periods
- **Humic+** is consistent throughout the year
- **Nurture** is consistent throughout the year

## Products NOT in the Application Guide Schedule

The following NZLA products do not appear in the monthly application schedule:
- Blockade (pre-emergent herbicide — different timing logic)
- BWC (PCBU-only)
- Fungus Pro / Posterity (disease-response products)
- NZLA Gold (home user fungicide)
- Worm Pellets
- Grub Shield (if separate from Grub+)
- Sedge Pro / MSF600 (herbicides)
- Grass seed products

These are applied situationally or seasonally outside the regular programme.
